Revenue Reports show gross sales, discounts, refunds, tax, and net revenue across any date range and branch.

## Overview

Key metrics:

| Metric | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Gross sales** | Total sold before discounts and refunds |
| **Discounts** | Sum of every discount applied |
| **Refunds** | Sum of refunded amounts |
| **Net revenue** | Gross – discounts – refunds |
| **Tax** | VAT or turnover tax collected |
| **Tips** | Optional; excluded from net revenue |

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Compare periods with matching operational conditions. Comparing a Friday to a Monday, or a rainy day to a public holiday, produces misleading conclusions.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not treat gross sales as revenue. Discounts, refunds, and tax must be netted out for a true revenue figure.
</Warning>
