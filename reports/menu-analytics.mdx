Menu Analytics ranks every product by sales, margin, popularity, and profitability. Combined they answer the classic menu question: what to keep, promote, reprice, or cut.

## Overview

Every product is classified using the Menu Engineering matrix:

| Popularity | Margin | Category | Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| High | High | **Star** | Feature |
| High | Low | **Plow horse** | Reprice or reduce cost |
| Low | High | **Puzzle** | Reposition |
| Low | Low | **Dog** | Cut |

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Review menu analytics monthly. Quarterly is too infrequent — seasonal shifts get missed.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not cut a product with high margin just because it sells slowly. Puzzles often need marketing, not removal.
</Warning>
