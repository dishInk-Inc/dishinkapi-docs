Staff Performance reports rank cashiers, waiters, and kitchen staff on sales, average check size, upsell rate, void rate, and refund rate.

## Overview

Metrics tracked per staff member:

- Total sales, transaction count, average check
- Upsell rate (modifier attach)
- Discount and void rates
- Refund rate
- Cash variance across shifts
- Hours worked and sales per hour

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Share top performers' patterns with the team. "Amina's average check is 25% higher — she offers a starter every time" is coaching gold.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not use void or refund rates in isolation. High rates may reflect a difficult section, not the staff member. Cross-reference with shift context.
</Warning>
