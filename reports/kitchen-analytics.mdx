Kitchen Analytics measures how fast your kitchen serves orders. Prep time, station throughput, bottlenecks, and dish-level timings all live here.

## Overview

Core metrics:

- Average prep time per item
- Time-in-status (new → preparing → ready → completed)
- Station throughput (grill, fry, bar, prep)
- Peak-hour heatmap
- Void reasons attributed to kitchen

</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Set realistic prep targets. Aspirational targets colour every ticket red and nobody trusts the colour codes.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not compare kitchen throughput between branches without matching menu complexity. A café with 10 items will always be faster than a full-service kitchen with 100.
</Warning>
