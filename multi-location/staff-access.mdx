Grant each staff member access to one, many, or all branches. Cashiers typically work one branch; area managers span several; owners access all.

## Overview

Access levels:

- **Home branch** — default branch on login
- **Additional branches** — optional, can switch
- **Group access** — owners and administrators only, sees group-level reports

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Limit multi-branch access to genuine multi-site staff. Excessive access creates audit noise.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not grant every manager group-level access. Group reports include sensitive metrics that not all managers need.
</Warning>
