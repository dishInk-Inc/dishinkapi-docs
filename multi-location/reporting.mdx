Group reporting rolls up sales, staff, kitchen, and menu data across every branch in one view — with drill-down per branch.

## Overview

Group reports include:

- Group revenue and net profit
- Best/worst performing branch
- Staff performance across branches
- Menu performance across branches
- Inventory movement across branches

<Note>
  Group mode requires **Owner** or **Administrator** role with group access.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Set weekly automatic email of the group revenue report to every branch manager. Peer comparison drives performance.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not compare branches with mismatched cost structures directly. High-rent branches will always show lower net without context.
</Warning>
