01

How do staff log into the system?

Staff log in using a secure PIN they create after a welcome email is sent when their account is created. Managers can reset PINs via email or set them manually from the staff management panel.

02

Can I control exactly what each staff member can see and do?

Yes. The role permission system has granular toggles across all modules, including admin page access, editing rights, transaction overrides, analytics, payroll, settings, LAN, and billing, all configurable per staff member or via reusable role templates.

03

Can I create reusable permission templates for different roles?

Yes. Role Templates let you configure a full permission set once, name it (e.g. Senior Waiter, Kitchen Lead), and apply it to any staff member instantly. Changes to a template update all staff using it.

04

How does the shift scheduler work?

Managers assign shifts to staff by selecting the date, start time, end time, and optional notes. Shifts appear in a weekly grid per staff member. Scheduled shifts turn active when the staff member clocks in and completed when they clock out.

05

How do staff clock in and out?

Staff clock in from any device using their secure PIN login. The live shift timer starts instantly and is linked to their POS orders and assigned tables. Managers can view the live shift feed in real time.

06

What performance data is tracked per staff member?

Revenue served, total orders taken, hours worked, top-selling item, and average order value, all pulled directly from POS data. Filterable by week, month, or all time with a progress bar toward a revenue target set in settings.

07

Can only owners add managers?

Yes. By design, only restaurant owners can appoint another staff member as a manager or owner. This prevents privilege escalation. A manager cannot elevate another staff member's role beyond their own.

08

Can I deactivate a staff member without deleting them?

Yes. Deactivating a staff member removes their access immediately but keeps all their historical data including shifts, performance, and payroll records intact. You can reactivate them at any time.
