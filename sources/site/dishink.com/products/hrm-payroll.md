05 Payroll Settings

## Set the rules. 
Payroll follows them.

Configure restaurant-wide payroll rules once and every payroll run uses them automatically.

OT Threshold

Set the daily hours before overtime kicks in. For example, hours 9+ are OT at your chosen multiplier.

Example: 8hrs threshold, 9th hour onwards = OT pay

OT Multiplier

Overtime hours are paid at a multiple of base rate. Set 1.5x for time-and-a-half or 2x for double time.

Example: K30/hr base x 1.5 = K45/hr OT rate

Monthly Proration

Monthly staff are paid proportionally to actual days worked, or always receive their full monthly salary.

Example: K3,000/mo / 26 days x 20 days = K2,308

06 Tip Distribution

## Fair tips. 
Automatically calculated.

Choose the distribution mode that fits your team. DishInk does the maths, no spreadsheets, no arguments after closing. All tips from POS sync to payroll automatically.

Equal Split (Pool Mode)

All tips are pooled and divided equally among active staff based on hours worked that session.

- Hours-weighted distribution
- Cash, card and mobile tips all pooled
- Auto-calculates each staff share

To Waiter Mode

Tips from POS orders link directly to the assigned waiter. Unassigned tips go to the pool.

- Direct tip ownership per waiter
- Unassigned tips auto-pooled
- Tip type breakdown: cash / card / pool

01

How is payroll calculated?

DishInk pulls data from completed shifts, tips recorded at the POS, active deductions, and salary advances. It calculates base pay, overtime, tips, commission, and bonuses, then deducts PAYE, NAPSA, advances and any custom deductions to produce the net pay.

02

Can I run payroll for all staff at once?

Yes. The bulk generate option runs payroll for every active staff member simultaneously for a selected period. You can also generate for a single staff member if needed.

03

What wage types are supported?

DishInk supports hourly, daily, and fixed monthly salary structures. You can also set overtime rates and commission percentages per staff member, all from the Wages tab.

04

How does tip distribution work?

You choose the mode in Settings. Equal Split divides the total tip pool equally among active staff by hours worked. To Waiter links tips from POS orders directly to the assigned waiter. Unassigned tips go to the pool.

05

How do salary advances work?

Staff submit a request through their dashboard with a reason. The manager approves or declines with one tap. Approved advances are recorded and automatically deducted from the next payroll run, no manual tracking needed.

06

What deduction presets are available?

DishInk includes Zambia Standard (PAYE + NAPSA), Basic (PAYE only), Full Package (PAYE + NAPSA + Medical + Uniform), and Hospitality (PAYE + Staff Meals + Uniform). You can also create custom deductions as fixed amounts or percentages.

07

Can staff download their own pay slips?

Yes. Each staff member has a personal earnings dashboard where they can view their current period pay, previous pay slips, tip breakdown, deductions, and advance history, and download PDF pay slips.

08

Can I export payroll data for accounting?

Yes. The Reports tab lets you export a full CSV of all payroll records for any period, including gross, net, hours, overtime, tips, deductions, and status, ready for accounting or tax submissions.
