## Got questions? We've got answers.

01

Where does the analytics data come from?

All analytics pull directly from your live POS data, every order, payment, kitchen ticket, and shift. There's nothing to set up or import. Data appears automatically as your team works.

02

How real-time is the data?

Revenue, menu, and staff data refresh when you hit the refresh button or change the date range. Kitchen data including overdue items updates in near real-time so managers can act fast.

03

Can I filter by date range?

Yes. Every analytics tab has a date range picker including today, this week, this month, or a custom range. Charts and totals update instantly when you change the range.

04

Can I export analytics data?

Yes. Every analytics module has a CSV export button. Export kitchen efficiency, menu performance, revenue breakdown, or staff leaderboard data for any date range, ready for accounting or reporting.

05

What is the on-time rate in kitchen analytics?

The on-time rate shows the percentage of items that were prepared within your target prep time. The default target is 85%. You can configure when the timer starts, either when the order is created or when it's sent to the kitchen.

06

What does void rate mean in menu analytics?

Void rate shows the percentage of ordered items that were voided or cancelled before being served. A high void rate on specific items may indicate kitchen issues, ordering errors, or items that need to be reviewed.

07

Can specific staff members see analytics?

Analytics access is permission-controlled. Owners and managers can see all four modules. Staff members can be granted access to specific analytics pages through the role permission system.

08

Does the staff leaderboard show individual performance?

Yes. The staff leaderboard shows revenue served, tips earned, hours worked, orders taken, and average revenue per hour, per staff member, for any period. Each card is expandable for full detail.
