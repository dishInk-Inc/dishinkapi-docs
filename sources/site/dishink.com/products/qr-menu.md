## Got questions? We've got answers.

Quick answers about DishInk QR Menus and Websites.

Reprints

01

Do customers need to download an app to view the menu?

No. Customers simply scan the QR code with their phone camera and the menu opens instantly in their browser. No app download, no login, no friction.

02

Can I update my menu without reprinting QR codes?

Yes. Your QR code always points to the same link. Update prices, add specials, or mark items sold-out from your dashboard and every scan reflects the change instantly no reprinting needed.

03

Can I use DishInk as just a menu without the full website?

Yes. Menu-Only Mode lets you use DishInk purely as a digital menu. Add a 'View Menu' button on your existing website that links to your DishInk menu link.

04

Can I design a custom QR code with my logo?

Yes. The built-in QR designer lets you change the QR colour, add your logo in the centre, and download a print-ready file. No third-party tools needed.

05

Can I generate different QR codes per table?

Yes. DishInk generates unique per-table QR codes. This allows the system to know which table is scanning and enables table-specific experiences.

06

Can I integrate a booking platform like OpenTable or Dineplan?

Yes. Paste your booking platform link in Settings and a Book Now button appears automatically on your menu. No code required. See our integrations page for the full list.

07

How many menu items can I add?

Starter plans include up to 15 items. Growth, Operations Suite and Enterprise plans include unlimited menu items across unlimited categories.

08

Can I add photos to my menu items?

Yes. Upload photos for each dish directly from your phone or desktop. Images are auto-optimised for fast loading on mobile networks.
