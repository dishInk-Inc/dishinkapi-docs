06 ZRA Smart Invoicing

## Tax compliance, built right in.

DishInk is integrated with the **Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA)** Smart Invoice system. Choose your tax method from Settings depending on your Revenue, receipts update automatically to match your selection.

Turnover Tax

For smaller businesses

Simplified tax calculated as a percentage of total sales turnover. No complex VAT calculations ideal for SMEs and growing restaurants.

- Auto-calculated on every sale
- Receipt shows TOT breakdown
- ZRA-compliant format
- No VAT registration required

ZRA Smart Invoice

Recommended

Full ZRA Smart Invoice integration. Every sale is submitted to ZRA in real time and returns a verified QR code that customers can authenticate.

- Real-time ZRA submission
- Verified QR on every receipt
- VAT-compliant breakdowns
- Audit-ready transaction logs
- Works with all DishInk receipt types

Switch tax method in one tap

Go to Settings, Billing and Tax to choose between Turnover Tax and Smart Invoice. All receipts update instantly.

[Open Settings](https://dishink.com/admin/settings)

01

Can I split bills between multiple customers?

Yes. Split equally, by item, or by custom amount. Each person can pay with a different method and get their own receipt.

02

Does DishInk process card payments?

No. DishInk records transactions alongside your own payment providers and card terminals. You keep full control of your money flow.

03

Does it work offline?

Yes. DishInk creates a backup LAN connection and offline syncing workflows so service never stops if the internet goes down your dveices simply talk to each other over your wifi connection even if you have no data & store it locally once your internet is stable it syncs to the cloud .

04

Can I run multiple POS terminals?

Yes. Multiple terminals operate simultaneously and stay fully in sync, ideal for bars, takeaway counters, and dining floors.

05

Is DishInk ZRA compliant?

Yes. Integrated with ZRA Smart Invoice. Choose between Turnover Tax and Smart Invoice from Settings, receipts update automatically.

06

Does the POS connect to the kitchen display?

Yes. Orders fire directly to the DishInk KDS the moment they are placed, no paper tickets, no manual relay.

07

Do You sale hardware and can your software run our current ones?

Yes we do sell hardware, reach out to get a quote and its not a mast to use our hardware our software's all works on any device even your existing ones

08

Can I generate batch invoices for multiple transactions?

Yes. DishInk lets you create batch invoices that consolidate all transactions for a given day, or for any custom date range you choose, into a single invoice.
