04 Tip Management

## Fair tips, 
automatically calculated.

Choose the distribution method that fits your team culture. DishInk does the calculation no spreadsheets, no arguments after closing.

Equal Split (Pool)

All tips pooled and divided equally among active staff based on hours worked that session.

To Waiter Mode

Tips from POS orders link directly to the assigned waiter. Unassigned tips go to the pool.

## Got questions? We've got answers.

01

How do staff clock in and out?

Staff clock in from any device using their secure staff login. The system records the exact timestamp and location, links it to their assigned tables, and starts the live shift timer automatically.

02

Can managers edit or override clock-in records?

Yes. Managers can edit shift logs, approve late clock-ins, manually close forgotten sessions, and add notes to any attendance record for full accountability.

03

How are tips distributed among the team?

You choose the mode in Settings. Equal Split divides the total pool among all active staff by hours. To Waiter links tips directly from POS orders to the assigned waiter. Both are automatic.

04

Does the system generate pay slips automatically?

Yes. At the end of each pay period, gross pay, overtime, tips, and deductions are calculated automatically. Staff can view and download PDF pay slips directly from their dashboard.

05

How do salary advances work?

Staff submit a request through their portal. The manager approves or declines it. Once approved, the advance amount is recorded and automatically deducted from the next payroll run.

06

What wage types are supported?

DishInk supports hourly, daily, and fixed monthly salary structures. You can also set custom overtime rates and commission percentages

07

Can staff see their own earnings history?

Yes. Each staff member has a personal earnings dashboard showing current period pay, previous pay slips, tips breakdown, deductions, and advance history.

08

Is the payroll data exportable?

Yes. Pay period summaries and individual pay slips can be exported as PDFs, making it straightforward to prepare reports for accounting or tax submissions.
