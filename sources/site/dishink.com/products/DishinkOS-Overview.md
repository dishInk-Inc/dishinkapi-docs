## Internet drops or Unstable. 
Dishink doesn't.

Three automatic safety nets. No setup, no manual switching, zero data loss.

01

Online

Full cloud sync. Everything is live, fast and in real time across all devices and our robust servers.

02

LAN Mode

Internet is gone but your router is still on. Devices talk to each other directly over your local network.

03

Fully Offline

No WiFi, no internet. Each device works completely independently, saving every action to local storage.

01

Is DishInk just for restaurants ?

No. DishInk OS is built for the hospitality industry as a whole — restaurants, cafes, bars, lodges, takeaways, and more. It's an operating system, so the modules adapt to how your venue operates.

02

Is DishInk one platform or multiple tools?

One platform. Every module, POS, KDS, Waiter App, Table Management, Inventory, Payroll, Analytics, QR Menu, and WhatsApp Orders, is built into a single connected system. Data flows automatically between modules with no syncing or integration required.

03

Do I need to use all the modules?

No. You can use only the modules relevant to your business. A cafe might only need QR code Menu, HRM, POS and analytics. A full-service restaurant might use everything.

04

Does it work on existing hardware?

Yes. DishInk runs on any device: phones, tablets, existing POS terminals

05

Does it work offline?

Yes. DishInk has three layers of protection: cloud sync when online, LAN mode when WiFi is available but internet bundles are not, and local storage when fully offline.

06

Can I manage multiple locations?

Yes. DishInk supports unlimited branches and terminals.

07

Is DishInk ZRA compliant?

Yes. DishInk integrates with ZRA Smart Invoice. You can choose between Turnover Tax and Smart Invoice from Settings.

08

How does pricing work?

DishInk is subscription-based with plans in both ZMW and USD. All core modules are included with no per-module fees.

09

Can DishInk connect to my accounting software?

Yes. DishInk integrates with select accounting software. This is mainly available on our Enterprise Plan, though Operations Suite customers may qualify case-by-case depending on setup. Contact our team to discuss your needs.

10

Can I try it before paying?

Yes. DishInk offers a 5-day free trial with no credit card required.
