02 Three Layers of Resilience

## Each layer is a safety net. 
You never fall out completely.

DishInk works at three levels. When one layer isn't available, the next one catches you automatically, with no setup, no manual switching, and no action from your team.

Normal

Layer 1 Online

Full cloud sync. Everything is live, fast and in real time across all devices and our servers.

- All devices synced to cloud
- Real-time updates everywhere
- Full transaction history
- Analytics and reporting live

LAN Mode

Layer 2 WiFi Only (LAN)

Internet is gone but your router is still on. Devices talk to each other directly over your local network.

- Orders fire to KDS over WiFi
- Table updates stay in sync
- Payments recorded locally
- Service continues uninterrupted

Offline

Layer 3 Fully Offline

No WiFi, no internet. Each device works completely independently, saving every action to local storage.

- Every action saved instantly locally
- Nothing waits for a connection
- Works on every device independently
- Auto-syncs when connection returns

Online

LAN Mode

Offline

Auto-Sync

**Automatic switching.** DishInk detects which layer is available and uses the best one. Your team does nothing.

04 Local Storage & Auto-Sync

## Every action saved instantly. 
Synced automatically.

Every order, payment, status update, and action is saved to the device's local storage the moment it happens, before it even attempts to reach the cloud. When connectivity returns, everything syncs automatically with no manual action needed.

Local First

Every action saves to the device instantly. Connection is never required to record it.

Auto-Sync

When internet or WiFi returns, all queued actions sync to the cloud automatically.

## Got questions? We've got answers.

![LAN Offline FAQ](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1556761175-5973dc0f32e7?q=80&w=2032&auto=format&fit=crop)

01

Does WiFi need internet for devices to talk to each other?

No. As long as your WiFi router is on, even with no internet, your devices can still communicate with each other over the local network. Orders fire to the kitchen, tables update, and payments record as normal.

02

What gets saved if there's no WiFi at all?

Everything. Every order, payment, status update, and action is saved directly to the device's local storage the moment it happens. Nothing waits for a connection. When WiFi or internet returns, everything syncs automatically.

03

Can I take payments when offline?

Yes. Cash payments work exactly as normal. Card and mobile money payments are recorded immediately and synced to the cloud when connectivity returns. Nothing is lost or delayed from the guest's experience.

04

Will I ever lose data if internet drops?

No. DishInk saves every action locally first, then syncs to the cloud. This means data is never dependent on an active internet connection to be recorded. The cloud gets it when it can and your local device always has it immediately.

05

Do I need to configure anything for offline mode?

Nothing. Offline protection is built in and always active. There are no settings to enable, no switches to flip, and no manual steps for your team. It just works.

06

What if the WiFi router itself loses power?

Each device works fully independently. They can no longer talk to each other but every device continues operating on its own, saving all actions locally. When the router comes back, devices reconnect and sync everything automatically.

07

How does my team know when they're offline?

There's a subtle status indicator, but in most cases your team won't notice. The interface works identically whether online or offline. Managers can check the LAN Monitor page for full visibility of connection status and queue.

08

How many devices can connect on one network?

Up to 10 devices on the same WiFi network. This covers all typical setups, including multiple waiter phones, KDS screens, POS terminals, and manager tablets all staying in sync.
