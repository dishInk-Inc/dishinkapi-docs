06 Delivery Your Way

## Delivery handled, 
your way.

DishInk integrates with Yango Delivery or any other delivery courier you use. Choose the mode that fits how your business operates.

We Handle Delivery

Restaurant arranges Yango

Customer provides their delivery location in the cart. You receive the order, confirm payment, then create the Yango delivery on your end. Customer pays the driver directly.

- Location step shown in cart
- Map pin included in message
- You create Yango order
- Customer pays driver directly

Customer Handles Delivery

Customer arranges Yango

Location step is skipped. After you confirm the order, the customer places their own Yango order. Ideal for restaurants that prefer customers to manage their own delivery.

- Location step skipped
- Simpler cart flow
- Customer places Yango order
- You prepare and package only

Switch delivery mode anytime in **Settings → WhatsApp Ordering**. Cart flow updates instantly.

01

Does the customer need to download an app?

No. Customers browse your QR menu on their phone browser, build their cart, and the order is sent directly through WhatsApp. Nothing to install.

02

How does the cart send the order?

When the customer taps 'Send Order', WhatsApp opens with a pre-filled message containing the full order item names, quantities, total, name, location, and scheduled time.

03

Do you process payments?

No. DishInk records the transaction. Customers pay directly to your mobile money or bank number which you set in Settings. You keep 100% of the money flow.

04

Can I choose who arranges the Yango delivery?

Yes. In Settings you choose 'We Handle' (you create the Yango order) or 'Customer Handles' (the customer places their own Yango order after confirmation). The cart flow adjusts accordingly.

05

Can I customise the WhatsApp message template?

Yes. In Settings you can write your own message template using tokens like {items}, {total}, {name}, {location}. Leave blank to use the smart default.

06

Can customers schedule delivery for a later time?

Yes. The details step includes a date picker and time slot selector. The scheduled time is included in the WhatsApp message automatically.

07

How do I track incoming orders?

All orders appear in the Orders dashboard. You can update delivery status, mark payments, log customer reviews, and filter by date or status all from one screen.

08

Does the cart look different on different restaurant websites?

Yes. The cart inherits your chosen website template's brand colors, typography, and style so it feels native to your restaurant, not a generic tool.
