04 Order Lifecycle

## Track every order from 
new to completed.

Every order moves through a clear lifecycle. Status updates sync in real-time across kitchen screens, POS terminals, and the waiter app and the table management view so everyone is always on the same page.

New

Order just placed. Appears on KDS instantly.

Preparing

Kitchen confirmed. Actively being cooked.

Ready

Food is plated. Waiting for pickup.

Completed

Order collected. Ticket cleared from screen.

**Real-time sync:** Status changes update instantly on KDS, POS, Waiter App, and the customer order board, Live Order Board and Table mananegment view port.

Does KDS replace paper tickets?

Yes. Every order fires directly to the kitchen screen the moment it's placed no printing, no misreads, no lost tickets. Everything is digital and real-time.

02

Can I use multiple kitchen screens?

Yes. Run as many KDS screens as you need — one per station, one for the head chef, and one for the customer-facing order board. All stay perfectly in sync.

03

Does it work offline?

Yes. DishInk KDS supports LAN (local network) operation. Even if the internet goes down, orders continue flowing between POS and kitchen screens and waiter app.

04

Can customers track their orders?

Yes. A QR code on every receipt or they can scan the qr code on there table and the system auto detects an active order and see real-time order progress from preparing to ready without bothering staff.

05

Does it connect to the POS and Waiter App?

Yes. KDS is fully integrated with DishInk POS and the Waiter App. Orders from any source appear instantly on the kitchen screen.

06

Can I see kitchen performance data?

Yes. Track average prep times, order throughput, peak hours, and station performance all from the DishInk analytics dashboard.
