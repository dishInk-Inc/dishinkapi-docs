# Source: https://dishink.com/refund-policy

We believe in fair and transparent billing. This policy explains exactly when and how refunds are issued for DishInk subscriptions, add-ons, and setup fees, so you always know where you stand.

### Key Points

- Monthly plans: full refund within 3 days
- Yearly plans: 10-month refund within 14 days
- Setup fees non-refundable once onboarding begins
- Add-ons non-refundable once work has started
- Support responds within 48 hours
- Refunds disbursed within 72 hours of approval

## Overview

At DishInk, we want you to feel confident subscribing to any of our plans. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are issued, how to request one, and what to expect once a request has been submitted. Please read this carefully before purchasing a subscription or add-on.

## Monthly Subscription Refunds

For all monthly plan subscribers (Starter, Growth, and Enterprise), we offer a full refund within 3 days of your billing date under the following conditions:

- You were charged by mistake or did not intend to subscribe; a full refund of that month's fee will be issued.
- You are unsatisfied with the service and have not yet completed onboarding; a full refund of that month's fee will be issued.
- Refund requests must be submitted within 3 calendar days of the billing date to be eligible.
- No partial refunds are issued for months already in progress beyond the 3-day window.
- If you cancel after the 3-day window, your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period with no refund issued.

## Yearly Subscription Refunds

For all annual plan subscribers, we offer a fixed 10-month refund if a request is submitted within 14 days of the billing date:

- Refund requests must be submitted within 14 calendar days of your annual billing date.
- If eligible, you will receive a refund equivalent to 10 months of the plan. The first 2 months are non-refundable regardless of how much of the plan you have used.
- This applies whether you subscribed by mistake, did not intend to renew, or are unsatisfied with the service.
- The 10-month refund amount is fixed and does not change based on usage; even if you have not used any of the plan, the refund remains capped at 10 months.
- Refunds on yearly plans are calculated at the monthly equivalent rate of your chosen plan.
- Requests submitted after the 14-day window are not eligible for a refund; your access will continue until the end of the annual period.

## Non-Refundable: Setup & Onboarding Fees

Setup and onboarding fees are non-refundable once onboarding has commenced. This includes:

- Any one-time setup fee charged at the time of subscription.
- In-person or remote onboarding sessions that have already been initiated or completed.
- Menu build-out work: once we have begun building or configuring your digital menu, the associated onboarding fee is non-refundable.
- If onboarding has not yet started and you cancel within your eligible refund window, the setup fee may be refunded at our discretion. Contact support to enquire.

## Add-On Refunds

Refunds on purchased add-ons are handled on a case-by-case basis:

- Add-ons are refundable if no work has been performed or the feature has not been activated within 3 days of payment.
- Once work has begun on an add-on (for example, a custom integration, branded asset, or additional configuration), the add-on is no longer eligible for a refund.
- To check whether your add-on qualifies, contact our support team as soon as possible after purchase.
- Add-ons purchased as part of a bundle are subject to the same conditions.

## How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please use one of the following channels:

- Contact Form: Submit a refund request via the contact form on our website at dishink.com/contact. Include your registered email address, your current plan, and a brief reason for the request.
- Email: Send your request directly to dishink26@gmail.com with the subject line 'Refund Request - \[Your Business Name\]'.
- Please include your account email, subscription type (monthly or yearly), date of the charge in question, and any relevant context.
- Do not dispute the charge with your bank before contacting us; we are happy to resolve it directly and chargebacks may result in account suspension.

## Response & Disbursement Timeline

Once your refund request has been submitted, here is what to expect:

- Our support team will review your request and respond within 48 hours on business days.
- If your refund is approved, disbursement will be initiated within 72 hours of approval.
- The time it takes for the refunded amount to appear in your account depends on your payment method: Mobile Money (MTN or Airtel) refunds typically reflect instantly or 1 day max ; card payments may take 2 to 7 business days depending on your bank or card provider.
- You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been processed on our end.
- DishInk is not responsible for delays caused by your bank or payment provider after disbursement has been initiated.

## Exceptions & Discretionary Refunds

We reserve the right to approve or decline refunds on a case-by-case basis outside of the conditions above. Exceptional circumstances such as extended platform outages, billing errors on our end, or other technical failures may qualify for refunds even outside the standard windows. Contact support to discuss your situation.

## Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated via email and through your admin dashboard. Continued use of DishInk after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

## Contact Information

For all refund-related queries, reach us at dishink26@gmail.com or via the contact form on our website. We aim to be fair, transparent, and responsive in every case.
