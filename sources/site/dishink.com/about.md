Our Story

## From a Frustrating QR Menu to The Next Problem.

![The moment that started DishInk](https://dishink.com/assets/foodontable-CiW4mUyp.webp)

February 2026. A birthday dinner with family. The food was great. The atmosphere was warm. Then the waiter handed us a QR code menu slow, difficult to navigate, and it felt like scrolling through a PDF from another decade. After a few minutes of frustration, we gave up and asked the waiter to just choose for us.

That experience sparked a question. Looking around at the wider industry, it became clear that many hospitality businesses had no website, outdated social pages, or no easy way for customers to even view their menu before leaving the house.

The problem wasn't small. It was everywhere. And it was solvable. So the team got to work.

What started as a simple digital menu quickly became something much bigger because the menu wasn't actually the real problem.

The Deeper Problem

## The Menu Was Just The Surface.

![Discovering the deeper problems in hospitality](https://dishink.com/assets/story2-GpZ4m1Ju.webp)

As the team worked closely with business owners, sat through busy service hours, and mapped how daily operations actually ran, a clear pattern emerged.

Orders were being lost between staff and the kitchen. Inventory was managed on paper or not at all. Payroll lived in spreadsheets. Managers were switching between multiple disconnected tools just to get through a normal day.

The deeper we looked, the more it became clear hospitality businesses didn't need another tool. They needed one platform, one system that connected everything, from the moment a customer orders to the moment payroll runs and thats how Dishink evolved into a platform with endless posibilities.

"Every hospitality business deserves powerful technology not just the ones that can afford expensive enterprise software, consultants, or agencies."
