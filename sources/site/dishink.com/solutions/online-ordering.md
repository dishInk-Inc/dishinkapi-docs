The Problem

## Most small businesses rely on WhatsApp and guesswork.

Manual ordering works when you have five customers. It breaks down when you have fifty.

Lost or forgotten orders

Orders buried in WhatsApp chats or missed during busy periods cost you money and reputation.

Delivery detail confusion

Addresses, times, and special requests scattered across different messages lead to constant mistakes.

No structured menu

Customers ask what do you have repeatedly because there is no menu they can browse independently.

No payment tracking

Manually tracking who has paid and who has not is time-consuming and easy to get wrong.

Difficult peak management

Without a system, busy periods become chaotic, too many orders and no clear prioritization.

Poor customer experience

Customers receive no confirmation, no tracking, and no professional communication, damaging trust.

Delivery and Pickup Control

## You decide how your business operates.

DishInk supports three fulfillment modes, use one, or offer all three depending on your business model.

Customer Delivery

Customers enter their accurate delivery location via GPS at checkout. Ideal if you use couriers or external delivery services for fulfillment.

- Customer enters delivery address
- Flexible for courier integration
- Delivery details in dashboard
- No manual address chasing

Business Delivery

You manage all deliveries internally. Assign and coordinate delivery from your dashboard, ideal for businesses with their own drivers.

- You manage all deliveries
- Assign internally from dashboard
- Full address visibility
- Delivery status control

Pickup

Customers collect from your location. Perfect for bakeries, cafes, and takeaway businesses where customers come to you.

- Customers collect in person
- Scheduled pickup time support
- No delivery coordination needed
- Ideal for local businesses
