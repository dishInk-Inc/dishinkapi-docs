The Problem

## Bars run fast. Problems hide in the chaos.

High energy venues are high risk for stock loss, billing errors, and staff accountability issues. Without the right tools, it's nearly impossible to stay in control.

Managing open tabs

Multiple open tabs across different tables and customers leads to confusion, disputes, and missed charges.

Fast-paced drink service

Orders placed verbally or on paper get missed, especially when the bar is busy and several staff are working.

Inventory losses

Without accurate tracking, stock disappears between deliveries and end-of-night counts, costing real money.

Manual reconciliation

End-of-night reconciliation takes too long and relies on memory, making it easy to miss discrepancies.

Flying blind on performance

No insight into what's selling, what customers love, how the kitchen is performing, or what your food costs and profits really look like.

Staff accountability

Without a digital system, it is hard to track who served what, identify problems, or manage performance.
