The Problem

## Challenges cafes face daily.

High foot traffic, perishable ingredients, and tight margins mean every inefficiency costs money.

Long peak-hour queues

Morning and lunch rushes create bottlenecks that slow service and frustrate customers.

Ingredient shortages

Running out of milk, syrup, or bread mid-service because stock was not tracked properly.

Manual stock tracking

Counting inventory by hand is slow, inaccurate, and takes staff away from customers.

Inconsistent recipes

Different staff use different amounts, leading to inconsistent products and unpredictable costs.

No profitability insight

You know your revenue but not which items are actually profitable after ingredient costs.

Why It Happens
