## Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about integrations and menu-only mode.

### What is Menu-Only Mode?

Menu-Only Mode allows you to use DishInk strictly as your digital menu without the full website features (like Home, About, or Gallery). This is perfect if you already have a website but need a better way to display your menu. When enabled, your DishInk link goes straight to the menu page. You can simply add a button on your existing website (e.g., 'View Menu') that links to your DishInk URL.

### Can I link back to my main website from DishInk?

Yes! In Menu-Only Mode, you can add External Links to your DishInk navigation. For example, you can add a 'Back to Home' or 'Visit Website' button that redirects customers from your digital menu back to your main business website. This creates a seamless experience between your site and your menu.

### How do I integrate third-party booking apps?

Integrating booking platforms like Dineplan, OpenTable, SevenRooms, or Resy is completely no-code. In your dashboard settings, simply paste the direct link to your booking widget or page. DishInk will automatically add a 'Book Now' button to your menu that opens your chosen booking platform. It's a simple link integration that works instantly.

### Do I need a third-party booking app?

Not necessarily. DishInk comes with a built-in booking request system that allows customers to send reservation requests directly to your dashboard and email. It works great for most restaurants. However, if you need advanced features like table management, floor plans, or deposit handling, integrating a specialized tool like Dineplan or OpenTable is a great optional upgrade.

### Can I embed the menu directly onto my website (iframe)?

We recommend linking to your DishInk menu rather than embedding it in an iframe. Linking ensures the best mobile experience, faster loading times, and proper QR scanning behavior. Just add a 'View Menu' button on your site that opens your DishInk link.
