## Runs on WiFi or data. 
Works with what you have.

All DishInk devices work on both WiFi and mobile data bundles for cloud connectivity. WiFi is required specifically for LAN mode the feature that lets devices communicate locally when your internet drops. We recommend WiFi as your primary connection for the best experience.

Hardware FAQ

## Common questions. 
Straight answers.

01

Do I have to use DishInk hardware?

Not at all. DishInk OS runs on any modern Android, Windows or IOS device. You can use your own phones, tablets, or terminals. Our hardware range exists for businesses that want a fully pre-configured, tested, and branded setup out of the box.

02

Do the devices come pre-configured with DishInk?

Yes. All hardware sourced through DishInk is pre-installed and configured with the DishInk OS during your onboarding process. Your team can start using it on day one without any technical setup.

03

What is the branded case that comes free?

Waiter tablets, the DishInk Tablet, and Waiter Phones come with a free protective case in DishInk's brand colors. It is a strong, durable case that protects the device during busy service and gives your team a professional, uniform look on the floor.

04

Does the POS Handheld GO really have a built-in printer?

Yes. The Handheld GO has an integrated receipt printer, so your staff can take an order and print a receipt or kitchen ticket right from their hand without needing a separate printer unit. Ideal for fast service, pop-up setups, and outdoor dining.

05

Can I use iPads or iOS devices?

Our primary range is Android and Windows -based, but if you prefer iOS devices like iPads, we can source them for you. Just mention it in your quote request and our team will provide options, pricing, and availability for iPad-based setups.

06

Do the devices need WiFi or can they run on mobile data?

All devices can run on either WiFi or mobile data bundles for cloud connectivity. However, WiFi is required for LAN mode — the feature that lets your devices talk to each other locally when the internet drops. We recommend having a WiFi network as your primary connection.

07

How do I find out exact pricing?

Use the Get a Quote button on any product. You will be directed through a short request form where you select the products and quantities you need and send us a message via WhatsApp. Our team will respond with current pricing, availability, and delivery options.

08

Why might device models vary?

We source the best available hardware at any given time. Because the hardware market changes — new models, supply shifts, stock availability — the exact tablet or phone model you receive may differ from what is shown. What stays the same is the category, capability, and DishInk software it runs. We always confirm the exact model before delivery.

09

Can I mix DishInk hardware with my own devices?

Yes. You can run DishInk OS across a mix of DishInk-supplied hardware and your own compatible Android, Windows, or iOS devices on the same network. All devices communicate seamlessly as long as they are on the same WiFi network.

10

Is delivery available outside Lusaka?

Yes. We can arrange delivery across Zambia and for international orders. Delivery timelines and costs will be confirmed in your quote response based on your location.
