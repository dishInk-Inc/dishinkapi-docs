## DishInk OS — POS System

### What is the DishInk POS and how does it work?

The DishInk POS is a **modern, touch-based Point of Sale system** designed specifically for food and beverage venues. Your cashiers and servers use it to take orders, apply modifiers, process payments, issue receipts, and manage tabs. It runs on any Android, windows, Mac Os , Ipad Os, and IOS touchscreen and connects directly to the Kitchen Display System and Waiter App in real time. Every order placed on the POS fires instantly to the kitchen no paper tickets, no shouting.

### Is the DishInk POS ZRA compliant?

Yes. The DishInk POS is fully **ZRA (Zambia Revenue Authority) compliant**. It is built to meet the requirements of the Smart Invoice system, meaning every sale is properly recorded and receipts are issued in the correct format. This keeps your business compliant without any manual tax calculations or separate ZRA software.

### Can the POS handle multiple terminals at the same time?

Yes. The DishInk POS supports **multiple simultaneous terminals** on the same network. You can have a main cashier POS, a bar terminal, and a server terminal all running at once, all synced in real time. Orders from any terminal appear on the KDS and in the backend dashboard immediately.

### Does the POS support modifiers and special requests?

Yes. The POS supports **full modifier management** — add-ons, removals, substitutions, cooking preferences, and special notes per item. For example: 'Burger — no onions, extra cheese, well done.' These modifiers fire with the order to the KDS so the kitchen sees exactly what to prepare.

### What payment methods does the POS support?

The POS supports **cash, card, mobile money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money), and split payments**. You can split a bill between multiple payment types on a single order — for example, part cash and part mobile money. The system records the payment method for every transaction, which flows through to your sales reports.

### Can I split bills on the POS?

Yes. The POS supports **bill splitting by item or by amount**, so a table of friends can each pay for their own dishes.

### Does the POS work with a receipt printer?

Yes. The POS integrates with **standard Bluetooth and network thermal receipt printers and more**. You can print customer receipts, kitchen order tickets (if not using a KDS screen), and end-of-shift reports. We support all major ESC/POS compatible printers.

### Can I apply discounts and promotions through the POS?

Yes. The POS supports **order-level discounts** — you can set a percentage discount, a fixed amount off, or a manager-approved override. Promotions like happy hour pricing or staff meals can be configured in your menu settings and applied automatically or manually at the point of sale.

## DishInk OS — Kitchen Display System (KDS)

### What is the Kitchen Display System (KDS)?

The KDS is a **screen-based order display system** that replaces printed kitchen tickets. When a server takes an order on the POS or Waiter App, it appears instantly on the KDS screen in the kitchen. Your kitchen team can see all active orders, their items, modifiers, and timing in one place — no paper, no lost tickets, no miscommunication.

### What screens can I run the KDS on?

The KDS runs on **any Android tablet or monitor with an Android TV stick**. You do not need specialist kitchen display hardware — a standard Android tablet mounted on the kitchen wall or pass works perfectly. We recommend mounting it in a waterproof or grease-resistant case in busy kitchen environments.

### Can I route different items to different kitchen stations?

Yes. The KDS supports **station-based order routing**. For example, grill orders go to the grill station screen, cold dishes go to the cold section screen, and drinks go to the bar screen. Each station only sees the items relevant to them, which keeps the kitchen organized and reduces confusion during peak service.

### How does the KDS handle order timing?

Every order on the KDS shows a **live timer** from the moment it was placed. Orders change color as they age — typically green when fresh, amber when approaching the target time, and red when overdue. This gives your kitchen team and managers an at-a-glance view of where delays are happening and which orders need to be prioritized.

### Can kitchen staff mark orders as ready on the KDS?

Yes. When a dish is ready, the kitchen team taps it on the KDS to mark it as **complete**. This can trigger a notification to the server that the food is ready for collection. Completed orders move off the active screen and are logged in the order history, giving you a full audit trail of every dish prepared.

### Does the KDS work if the internet drops?

Yes. The KDS operates over your **local network (LAN)**, so it continues to receive and display orders even when the internet is down. As long as your POS, Waiter App, and KDS are on the same local network, everything keeps working without interruption.

## DishInk OS — Waiter App

### What is the DishInk Waiter App?

The Waiter App is a **mobile ordering tool for your front-of-house team**. Servers use it on a smartphone or tablet to take orders tableside, send them directly to the kitchen via the KDS, and manage their tables — all without walking to a POS terminal. It eliminates order-taking errors, speeds up service, and frees up your main POS for payments.

### What devices does the Waiter App run on?

The Waiter App runs on **any smartphone or tablet**. Your servers can use their own personal devices if you prefer, or you can provide dedicated handheld devices. Any modern device with your local WiFi works — no specialist hardware required.

### Can servers see the full floor plan and table status in the Waiter App?

Yes. The Waiter App includes a **live floor plan view** showing the status of every table — open, occupied, waiting for food, bill requested, and more. Servers can see which tables are theirs, what is outstanding, and which tables need attention, all from their handheld device.

### Can servers add modifiers and special requests from the Waiter App?

Yes. The Waiter App supports **full modifier and special request entry** — the same as the main POS. Servers can add cooking preferences, remove ingredients, add extras, and leave item-level notes that fire straight to the relevant KDS station with the order.

### Can a server send orders to the kitchen directly from the Waiter App?

Yes. When a server finalizes an order on the Waiter App, it fires **directly and instantly to the KDS** in the kitchen — no need to walk to the POS and re-enter it. Orders go from table to kitchen in seconds with zero re-keying.

### Can the Waiter App handle multiple tables at once?

Yes. Each server can manage **multiple tables simultaneously** from the Waiter App. They can switch between tables, add to existing orders, view what has been ordered, and check what is still outstanding — all without leaving the floor.

## DishInk OS — Table Management

### What does Table Management include?

Table Management gives you a **live digital floor plan** of your venue. You can see every table's current status at a glance — available, occupied, waiting for food, awaiting bill, or reserved. It integrates with the POS and Waiter App so any order or payment activity updates the floor plan in real time.

Yes. Each table has its own **running tab** — you can add items to it over the course of a meal, view the current total at any time, and close the tab when the guest is ready to pay. Open tabs are visible in both the POS and the Waiter App.

### Does Table Management support reservations?

Yes. You can **block tables as reserved** for upcoming bookings directly from the floor plan. Reservations show the guest name and time, so your front-of-house team always knows which tables to hold. Yes. If a group moves tables or two groups want to sit together, you can **merge tables** in the system so their orders and bill are combined. You can also **move an open tab** from one table to another if guests relocate during their visit.

## DishInk OS — Inventory & Food Costing

### How does Inventory Management work in the OS?

The DishInk Inventory system tracks your **stock levels in real time**. When you receive deliveries, you log them as stock-in. When items are sold, the system automatically deducts the correct ingredients based on your **recipe-linked menu items**. So if a burger sells, the system deducts the bun, patty, lettuce, and sauce from your stock — automatically, with no manual counting required after every service.

### What is recipe-based inventory deduction?

Recipe-based deduction means you link each menu item to a **recipe** that specifies its exact ingredients and quantities. When that item is sold through the POS or Waiter App, the system automatically deducts the right amounts from your stock. This gives you **real-time, accurate stock levels** at all times without any manual intervention between services.

### Can I track food cost and profit margins per dish?

Yes. The Inventory module includes **food costing tools** that calculate the cost of each dish based on its recipe and your current ingredient prices. You can see your **cost per plate, gross margin, and profitability** for every menu item. This helps you identify which dishes are most profitable and where you might be over-portioning or underpricing.

### Does the system alert me when stock is running low?

Yes. You can set **minimum stock thresholds** for any ingredient. When stock falls below your threshold, you receive an alert in your dashboard. This prevents you from running out of key ingredients mid-service and helps your team know when to place orders with suppliers.

### Can I track waste and over-portioning?

Yes. You can log **waste entries** manually — for example, if a dish is spoiled, sent back, or dropped. The system tracks waste over time so you can spot patterns, identify high-waste items, and take action to reduce losses. Combined with your recipe deductions, any discrepancy between theoretical and actual stock usage highlights potential over-portioning or theft.

### Can I manage multiple suppliers in the system?

Yes. You can add **multiple suppliers** to your inventory system and tag ingredients to their respective supplier. This makes it easy to log deliveries by supplier, track what you buy from each one, and compare costs if you use multiple sources for the same ingredient.

## DishInk OS — HRM, Payroll & Attendance

### What does the HRM module include?

The HRM (Human Resource Management) module covers your entire workforce: **employee profiles, roles and permissions, contract details, salary structures, leave management, and performance tracking**. Every staff member who uses the OS has a profile in the HRM with their role, access level, and employment details.

### How does the Payroll module work?

The Payroll module is designed for **one-tap payroll processing**. Once your staff's salary structures, working hours, and attendance records are in the system, generating payroll is a single action. The system calculates base pay, overtime, tips, and deductions automatically — giving you a payroll summary ready to review and approve. No spreadsheets, no manual calculations.

### Does the system handle tips and tip allocation?

Yes. The Payroll module supports **tip tracking and allocation**. Tips collected through the POS can be distributed equally across all staff on shift, proportionally based on hours worked, or allocated to specific servers. This keeps tip distribution transparent and fair, and it is all recorded for your payroll records.

### How does Staff Attendance work?

Staff Attendance uses a **PIN-based clock-in and clock-out system**. Each staff member has a unique PIN they enter at the start and end of their shift on any OS terminal. The system records their exact clock-in and clock-out times, calculates total hours worked per shift and per pay period, and flags late arrivals or early departures automatically.

### Can I manage staff shifts and scheduling?

Yes. The HRM module includes **shift management** tools. You can create shift schedules, assign staff to specific shifts, and the system will track whether they clock in as scheduled. Managers can view who is currently on the floor at any time, and the shift log feeds directly into payroll calculations.

### Can I track staff advances and deductions?

Yes. The Payroll module supports **salary advances and deductions**. If a staff member takes a cash advance, you log it in the system and it is automatically deducted from their next payroll. The same applies to any other agreed deductions — keeping payroll accurate without manual adjustments.

### Does the system support role-based access and permissions?

Yes. Every staff member is assigned a **role** — Cashier, Waiter, Kitchen Staff, Supervisor, or Manager. Each role has a defined set of **permissions** that controls what they can see and do. A cashier cannot access payroll. A waiter cannot issue refunds without manager approval. A kitchen display operator sees only orders. This keeps your system secure and your operations structured.

### Can I track KPIs and staff performance?

Yes. The HRM module tracks key performance indicators per staff member, including **orders processed, average order value, items sold, attendance rate, and punctuality**. Managers can use these metrics during staff reviews or to identify top performers and those who may need additional training.

## QR Code Digital Menu System

### How does the QR code menu system work?

You create **unique QR codes for each table** via the admin dashboard. When customers scan the code with their phone camera, they instantly see your digital menu with photos, prices, and descriptions. **No app download needed** and it works on any smartphone.

### Are DishInk QR codes dynamic or static?

All DishInk QR codes are **fully dynamic**. With static QR codes, if you change your URL, business name, or menu, the code breaks and you have to reprint. With DishInk's dynamic QR codes, the code never changes — it always points to the same permanent link. You can rebrand, swap templates, or update any content and your existing printed QR codes still work. **You only ever need to reprint if you physically damage or lose the printed code**.

### Can I create QR codes for different tables?

Yes. You can generate **table-specific QR codes** — Table 1, Table 2, VIP Booth, etc. Table-based QR assignment is available on **Growth, Operations, and Enterprise** plans.

### How do I download and print the QR codes?

In your dashboard, go to the **QR Codes** section. For each table, click **'Download as PNG'**. Print these on table tents, stickers, or display stands. We recommend laminating them for durability.

### What if a customer does not have a smartphone?

Your DishInk website is also accessible via a **shareable URL** like dishink.com/r/yourrestaurant. Share this on WhatsApp, social media, or Google. Customers can view your menu on any device.

### Can I have a custom-branded QR code with my logo?

Yes. Custom QR codes with your logo are available on the **Growth, Operations, and Enterprise** plans.

We offer four plans: **Starter (K399/mo or K4,300/yr)** for a basic digital menu. **Growth (K599/mo or K6,300/yr)** for a premium website with WhatsApp ordering. **Operations Suite (K4,500/mo or K50,000/yr)** for a full restaurant OS with POS, KDS, inventory, and payroll. **Enterprise (K9,500/mo or K100,000/yr)** for multi-location chains needing everything. All plans include a free 5-day trial.

### What are the setup fees?

Setup fees are **optional** for Starter (K450) and Growth (K850) — you can self-setup for free. They are **required** for the **Operations Suite (from K5,000)** and **Enterprise (Custom)** as these cover comprehensive on-site configuration, hardware setup, and staff training.

### What is the difference between the Operations Suite and Enterprise?

The **Operations Suite** is for single or dual-location venues needing a full operational system — 2 locations and all OS modules included. **Enterprise** is for multi-location groups needing up to 5 locations, plus a centralized dashboard, custom domain, business email, dedicated account manager, complimentary photo shoot, and priority same-day support.

### What are the optional add-ons?

Any plan can be enhanced with: **Professional Photo Shoot (K850)**, **Custom Domain Setup (K2,000)**, **Accounting Integration (K1,500)**, **Extra Location — Operations (K750/mo)**, **Extra Location — Enterprise (K1,500/mo)**, **Custom Template Design (from K4,000)**, and **Rush Setup (+35% of setup fee)**.

### Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can **upgrade or downgrade** your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. You can also switch between monthly and yearly billing anytime.

## Payments & Billing (Zambia)

### How do I pay for my DishInk subscription?

Currently, payments are handled **directly between you and DishInk**. We accept **MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and bank transfers**. Once you sign up and select a plan, our team will reach out to confirm and arrange payment. You can choose to pay **monthly or yearly**.

### Will you add automated payments in the future?

Yes. We are actively working on **automated payment processing** so you can subscribe, pay, and manage billing entirely online. We will notify all users as soon as this is available.

### When do I pay the setup fee?

For Starter and Growth, the setup fee is optional and paid before our team begins your onboarding. For Operations Suite and Enterprise, the required setup fee is paid upfront to begin configuration and training. If you self-setup (Starter/Growth only), there is no setup fee at all.

### Are there hidden costs or long-term contracts?

No hidden costs and no long-term contracts. The price you see is what you pay. Your subscription includes hosting, SSL, and customer support. Cancel anytime with no penalties.

### What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel at any time by contacting support. Your services remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that, they go offline but **your data is safely stored for 90 days** in case you want to reactivate.

## Customization & Branding

### What branding can I apply to my site?

You can upload your **restaurant logo**, set your **brand colors**, add social media links, and customize your homepage hero, about text, and gallery. All plans support full branding customization.

### Will DishInk branding appear on my site?

On **Starter and Growth**, a small DishInk badge appears in the footer. On **Operations Suite and Enterprise**, the DishInk badge is removed. On Enterprise, your own restaurant logo is displayed when your link is shared or previewed.

### Can I connect a custom domain?

Custom domain setup is available as an optional add-on for Starter, Growth, and Operations plans at **K2,000 one-time (or $200 USD)**. It is **included for free with the Enterprise plan**.

### Can I add social media links?

Yes. In **Settings → Social Links**, add URLs for Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Twitter, and WhatsApp. These appear as icons in your website footer.

### Can I have a custom menu banner and featured dishes section?

Yes. Both are available on the **Growth, Operations, and Enterprise plans**.
