## Multi-Location Pricing

If you operate more than one venue, here is how additional locations are priced:

**Operations Suite** — 2 locations included. Add up to 3 more at K750/mo (or K9,000/yr) each. Beyond 5 total locations, you must upgrade to Enterprise.

**Enterprise** — Up to 5 locations included. Add unlimited further locations at K1,500/mo (or K18,000/yr) each.

A "location" counts as a different physical address, a different concept under one brand, or a different brand under one company. [Contact the team](https://dishink.com/contact) for a custom multi-location quote.
