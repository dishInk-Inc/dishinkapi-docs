## The Challenge: What holds full-service restaurants back

Running a full-service restaurant means coordinating waiters, kitchen staff, cashiers, and managers simultaneously — often during a packed service. When the systems supporting those teams don't talk to each other, problems multiply fast.

- **Paper-based orders** — Notepads get lost, misread, or arrive late to the kitchen during a busy service when every minute counts.
- **Kitchen miscommunication** — Waiters and kitchen staff work off different information, leading to wrong orders, forgotten items, and frustrated guests.
- **Slow billing** — Generating bills, applying discounts, and splitting checks takes too long and creates bottlenecks right when tables need to turn over.
- **Poor inventory visibility** — You don't know how much stock you have until an ingredient runs out mid-service, and without recipe tracking, your real food cost is just a guess.
- **Manual payroll and scheduling** — Building shift schedules and calculating wages, tips, and deductions by hand every month is time-consuming and error-prone.
- **No unified analytics** — Without kitchen, menu, staff, and sales data in one place, you end up making decisions based on gut feeling instead of facts.

## How DishInk solves it

DishInk connects every stage of your restaurant's operation so that each action — taking an order, preparing a dish, processing a payment — flows automatically to the next.

**Waiter-to-kitchen sync** — Orders placed on the Waiter App fire to the Kitchen Display System in seconds, with modifiers and special requests included. No shouting, no paper tickets.

**Integrated billing and split payments** — Your POS handles full bills, item-level splits, discounts, and mixed payment methods (cash, card, mobile money) from a single screen. ZRA Smart Invoice compliance is built in.

**Real-time manager visibility** — Managers see live table status, open orders, and staff activity from any device, without walking the floor.

**Recipe-based inventory deductions** — Every item sold automatically deducts the correct ingredients from stock. You always know your real food cost and current stock levels without a manual count.

**Unified analytics** — Sales, kitchen performance, menu profitability, staff activity, and food costs all update in real time in one analytics dashboard.

**Offline resilience** — DishInk's three-layer offline mode (cloud → LAN → local storage) means your service never stops when the internet does.

## A typical service flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer is seated">
    Table status updates to Occupied. The guest scans the table QR code to browse the digital menu on their phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Waiter takes the order">
    Your waiter opens the Waiter App, selects the table, and adds items with modifiers and special requests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kitchen receives the ticket">
    The order appears instantly on the KDS, routed to the correct station — grill, cold section, or bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kitchen prepares and updates">
    Kitchen staff mark items as Preparing, then Ready. Status updates in real time across all devices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer tracks progress">
    The guest can follow their order status via the table QR code on their own phone — no need to flag down the waiter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bill generated and paid">
    The cashier or waiter opens the bill on the POS, applies any discounts, splits items if needed, and processes payment. A ZRA-compliant receipt is issued automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inventory updates automatically">
    Every ingredient in every dish sold is deducted from stock based on your pre-configured recipes. No manual counting required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analytics update in real time">
    Sales, food cost, table turnover, and kitchen timing data flow straight into your analytics dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can DishInk work offline during a power or internet outage?">
    Yes. DishInk uses a three-layer offline model: cloud sync when online, LAN-based operation when the internet drops, and local device storage as a last resort. Your POS, KDS, and Waiter App continue taking and routing orders without interruption. Everything syncs automatically when connectivity is restored.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I run DishInk on my existing tablets and POS hardware?">
    Yes. DishInk is hardware-agnostic and runs on any modern Android or iOS tablet, touchscreen monitor, or POS terminal. You don't need to buy new hardware from us — if your devices meet the minimum spec, DishInk will work on them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can different staff members have different access levels?">
    Yes. DishInk uses role-based access control. Managers, waiters, cashiers, kitchen staff, and administrators each get only the permissions they need. Sensitive actions like applying discounts or viewing financial reports can be restricted to managers only.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is DishInk compliant with ZRA Smart Invoice requirements?">
    Yes. The DishInk POS is fully ZRA Smart Invoice compliant. Every sale is properly recorded and receipts are issued in the correct format automatically, keeping your business compliant without any manual tax calculations or separate ZRA software.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does DishInk support split billing for tables?">
    Yes. The POS supports bill splitting by individual item or by amount, so a table of guests can each pay for their own dishes or split the total however they choose. It handles mixed payment methods — cash, card, and mobile money — on the same bill.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
