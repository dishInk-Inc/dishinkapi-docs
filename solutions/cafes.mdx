Cafés, coffee shops, bakeries, dessert shops, and juice bars share a common problem: tight margins, perishable ingredients, and peak-hour rushes that leave no room for error. DishInk connects your counter, your kitchen, and your back office into one platform — so you can serve customers faster, track every ingredient against every recipe, and know exactly which items on your menu are actually making you money.

## The challenge: fast service, invisible costs

High foot traffic, perishable ingredients, and slim margins mean every inefficiency at a café costs real money. The most damaging problems tend to be:

- **Long peak-hour queues** — Morning and lunch rushes create bottlenecks that slow service, frustrate customers, and limit how many covers you can do.
- **Ingredient shortages mid-service** — Running out of milk, syrup, or bread because stock wasn't tracked properly is both embarrassing and costly.
- **Inconsistent recipes** — Different baristas using different quantities lead to inconsistent products and unpredictable costs from one day to the next.
- **No profitability insight** — You know your revenue but not which items are actually profitable after ingredient costs are factored in.
- **Manual stock tracking** — Counting inventory by hand is slow, inaccurate, and pulls staff away from customers.

## How DishInk solves it

DishInk gives your café the operational backbone that most café POS systems don't provide — connecting every sale to its ingredients, tracking costs in real time, and keeping your team running efficiently from open to close.

**Fast counter checkout** — The DishInk POS is touch-optimised for quick counter service. Process orders fast with modifier support (e.g. "oat milk, no sugar"), apply discounts, and handle cash, card, and mobile money without switching tools.

**Recipe-based inventory deductions** — Every item sold automatically deducts the exact ingredients from stock based on your configured recipes. A double espresso uses 18g of beans and 30ml of milk. DishInk tracks that deduction so you always know your real stock level.

**Food cost tracking** — See the actual cost per menu item based on current ingredient prices. When ingredient costs change, your food cost reports update automatically — no spreadsheet gymnastics required.

**Kitchen Display System** — Orders from the counter fire to the KDS instantly, keeping your baristas and kitchen staff organised and reducing errors during busy periods.

**Waste recording** — Log wastage (spoiled ingredients, prep errors, overproduction) directly in DishInk so your stock counts and cost reports stay accurate.

**Staff scheduling and attendance** — Build shift rosters, track clock-ins via PIN, and generate payroll with automatic wage calculations.

<Tip>
  If you run a bakery or dessert shop with limited daily quantities, use DishInk's low-stock alerts to get notified when an ingredient or finished item drops below a threshold you set — before you run out during service.
</Tip>

## A typical service flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Order placed at the counter">
    Your cashier or barista takes the order on the POS. Modifiers (milk type, size, extras) are added in seconds. Payment is processed via cash, card, or mobile money.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ticket appears on the KDS">
    The order fires to the KDS screen behind the counter or at the prep station, showing the item, modifiers, and any special instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drinks and food prepared to recipe standards">
    Your team follows the configured recipe, maintaining consistent quality and keeping ingredient usage in line with your cost targets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order status updates for the customer">
    The customer can see their order progress on the collection screen. When it's ready, their number moves to the Ready column.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inventory deducted automatically">
    The moment the sale is confirmed, every ingredient in that item is deducted from stock. No manual counting, no end-of-day guesswork.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data updates in real time">
    Sales figures, food costs, and profitability data update instantly in your analytics dashboard. You always know where you stand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key benefits

- **Faster service during peak hours** — A streamlined POS checkout and KDS-driven kitchen coordination dramatically reduce wait times when the morning rush hits.
- **Real-time stock visibility, zero mid-service surprises** — Recipe-linked inventory deductions mean you get low-stock alerts before you run out — not after your first customer is turned away.
- **True food cost visibility** — Know the actual margin on every item you sell. Price new products correctly, spot unprofitable lines, and make smarter menu decisions.
- **Consistent quality, lower waste** — Standardised recipes in DishInk mean every staff member makes every item the same way. Less over-pour, less over-portion, less waste — and lower costs.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I set up recipes with exact ingredient quantities per menu item?">
    Yes. DishInk's inventory module lets you define a recipe for every item on your menu — the exact ingredients and quantities used per serving. Every time that item is sold, those quantities are automatically deducted from your stock levels. This gives you accurate real-time stock counts and a true per-item food cost.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I track ingredients that are used in multiple products?">
    Yes. A single ingredient (for example, whole milk or espresso beans) can appear in multiple recipes. DishInk tracks total consumption across all products that use it, so your stock count is always accurate regardless of how many menu items share the same ingredient.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can different staff have different access levels at the POS?">
    Yes. DishInk supports role-based permissions. Baristas can process orders and modifiers; managers can apply discounts, view cost reports, and edit the menu. You control what each role can and cannot access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does DishInk work offline if the internet drops?">
    Yes. DishInk's three-layer offline mode (cloud → LAN → local storage) keeps your POS and KDS running during internet outages. Orders continue flowing to the kitchen, payments are processed, and everything syncs automatically when the connection is restored.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I manage a menu that changes daily (like a bakery)?">
    Yes. You can update menu availability, toggle items on and off, and adjust stock quantities from your dashboard at any time — no reprinting required. If you use a QR digital menu, it updates immediately for customers the moment you make a change.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
