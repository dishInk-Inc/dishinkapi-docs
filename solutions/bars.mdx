Bars, lounges, sports bars, and nightclubs operate in a world of open tabs, multi-round orders, and high-speed service where the difference between a profitable night and a chaotic one comes down to how well your systems keep up. DishInk gives you real-time operational visibility across every shift — open tabs stay organised, drink inventory is tracked automatically, staff are held accountable, and end-of-night reconciliation takes minutes instead of hours.

## The challenge: fast-paced service, hidden losses

High-energy venues are high-risk for stock loss, billing errors, and staff accountability gaps. Without the right tools, it's nearly impossible to stay in control when the venue is packed.

- **Managing open tabs** — Multiple tabs open across different tables and customers leads to confusion, missed charges, and disputes at the end of the night.
- **Fast-paced verbal ordering** — Orders placed verbally or on paper get missed, especially when several staff are working the floor simultaneously.
- **Inventory losses** — Without accurate tracking, stock disappears between deliveries and end-of-night counts, costing real money with no clear explanation.
- **Manual end-of-night reconciliation** — Closing out at the end of a shift takes too long and relies on memory, making it easy to miss discrepancies.
- **No staff accountability** — Without a digital system, it is hard to track who served what, identify problems, or address performance issues with evidence.
- **Invisible performance data** — No insight into what's selling, which nights are most profitable, or what your real margins look like after a busy Saturday.

## How DishInk solves it

DishInk gives your bar or nightclub the operational visibility most till systems don't provide — not just payment processing, but full tab management, inventory movement tracking, staff accountability, and real-time reporting across every shift.

**Open tab management** — Open a tab for a customer or table at the start of the night and keep adding rounds to it. Tabs stay clearly organised so nothing gets missed, and the balance is always visible at a glance.

**Split billing** — When a group is ready to settle up, DishInk handles splits by item or by amount cleanly. Multiple payers, multiple payment methods — all handled from the same screen.

**Waiter App for floor ordering** — Waiters record orders digitally on their devices and send them directly to the bar's KDS. No shouting, no paper, no missed rounds.

**Bar KDS** — The Kitchen Display System (used at the bar as well as the kitchen) receives drink orders the moment they're placed. Bartenders see every active order in sequence, reducing wait times and service errors.

**Drink inventory tracking** — Every bottle, spirit, and mixer is tracked. Each sale deducts from stock automatically, so your inventory counts are always accurate. Spot losses immediately — you'll know within a single shift.

**Manager dashboards** — Monitor the live floor, open tabs, sales, and staff activity from any device without being physically present at every station.

**Automated end-of-night reports** — Sales, inventory movements, staff performance, and payment breakdowns generate automatically. Reconciliation becomes a review, not a reconstruction.

## A typical service flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer opens a tab">
    A table is assigned and a tab is opened at the POS or via the Waiter App. The tab is active and visible to all staff.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Waiter records orders digitally">
    As the night progresses and rounds are ordered, your waiter logs each round via the Waiter App — drinks, specials, food — all added to the same tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bar staff receive tickets on the KDS">
    Every drink order appears instantly on the bar's KDS screen. Bartenders work through the queue in order, with full visibility of what's been requested and by which table.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drinks prepared and served">
    Bartenders mark items as Ready on the KDS. Stock is deducted automatically from the inventory at the moment of sale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bill settled or split">
    At the end of the night (or whenever the customer is ready), the cashier or waiter opens the tab, applies any outstanding charges, and processes payment. Split between multiple payers if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reports update automatically">
    Sales totals, inventory movements, staff performance, and payment breakdowns are all updated in real time. End-of-night reconciliation is a quick review, not a manual exercise.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key benefits

- **Tighter stock control, fewer losses** — Every drink served is recorded and deducted from stock automatically. Unexplained inventory shrinkage becomes immediately visible — and easier to investigate.
- **Full staff accountability** — Every order is linked to the staff member who placed it. Managers can see exactly who served what, when, and on which table — making performance reviews and dispute resolution straightforward.
- **Faster service, happier customers** — Digital orders via the Waiter App and bar KDS eliminate missed rounds and reduce the time between a customer's order and their drink arriving.
- **End-of-night in minutes, not hours** — Automated sales reports, inventory snapshots, and payment breakdowns mean your closing routine is a quick review instead of a manual reconstruction.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I manage multiple open tabs across the venue at the same time?">
    Yes. DishInk supports multiple simultaneous open tabs — one per table or one per customer. You can add rounds to any tab throughout the night, view all open tabs from the manager dashboard, and close them individually or in bulk at the end of service.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can DishInk track spirits and bottles at the ingredient level?">
    Yes. You can set up your inventory with bottles, mixers, and garnishes as individual stock items. Every drink sold deducts the correct amount based on your recipe settings. This lets you track usage accurately and spot discrepancies between what was sold and what's left on the shelf.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can staff have different access levels — for example, bartenders versus managers?">
    Yes. DishInk uses role-based access control. Bartenders and waiters can take orders and process payments on their permitted terminals. Only managers can apply discounts, view financial reports, or edit the menu. PIN-based login ensures the right person is taking each action.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does DishInk work offline during internet outages?">
    Yes. DishInk's LAN offline mode keeps your POS, Waiter App, and KDS running on your local network even if the internet goes down. Orders continue flowing, payments are processed, and all data syncs automatically when the connection is restored. Your service is never interrupted by connectivity issues.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use DishInk for event nights and VIP table bookings?">
    Yes. DishInk includes a booking and reservations module that handles event night bookings, VIP table requests, bottle service, and private hire. Bookings arrive directly in your dashboard inbox and link to your live table management, so your floor plan always reflects confirmed reservations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
