If you currently take orders through WhatsApp messages, Instagram DMs, or phone calls, you already know what happens when you get busy: orders get buried, delivery details go missing, and customers follow up constantly asking for confirmation. DishInk gives home bakers, caterers, cake businesses, and takeaway shops a proper ordering system without the cost and complexity of a full eCommerce platform. You get a branded menu page, a structured WhatsApp ordering link, a delivery and pickup scheduler, and a dashboard where every order is organised and trackable from the moment it arrives.

## The challenge: manual ordering breaks down at scale

Manual ordering works when you have five customers a week. It becomes unmanageable when you have fifty.

- **Lost and forgotten orders** — Orders buried in WhatsApp threads or missed during busy periods cost you money, damage your reputation, and erode customer trust.
- **Delivery detail confusion** — Addresses, times, and special requests scattered across different messages lead to constant mistakes and frustrated customers.
- **No structured menu** — Customers message repeatedly asking "what do you have?" because there is no menu they can browse independently before ordering.
- **No payment tracking** — Manually tracking who has paid, who is outstanding, and which order matches which payment is time-consuming and easy to get wrong.
- **Poor customer experience** — Customers receive no order confirmation, no tracking update, and no professional communication — which makes your business feel informal even when the food is excellent.

## How DishInk solves it

DishInk turns your food business into a structured ordering system. Customers browse a menu, place a complete order in one step, choose how and when they want it — and you manage everything from a clean dashboard.

**Branded online storefront** — Your menu page carries your logo, brand colours, photos, and product categories. It looks professional, it's mobile-friendly, and you can share the link anywhere — Instagram bio, WhatsApp status, or a printed card.

**WhatsApp ordering link** — Share your DishInk menu link via WhatsApp. Customers browse your full menu, build their cart, and submit a structured order directly through the link. No more back-and-forth message chains. No more manually typed order summaries.

**Scheduled orders** — Customers choose their preferred delivery or pickup date and time when they place the order. Everything in your dashboard is automatically organised by schedule, so you can plan your production and prep without a separate system.

**Three delivery modes** — Choose the fulfilment model that suits your business:

- **Customer Delivery (GPS)** — Customers enter their delivery location via GPS at checkout. Ideal if you use couriers or external delivery services.
- **Business Delivery** — You manage all deliveries internally. Assign and coordinate from your dashboard, ideal for businesses with their own drivers.
- **Pickup** — Customers collect from your location at a scheduled time. Perfect for bakeries, cake businesses, and local takeaways.

**Order dashboard** — Every incoming order appears in your dashboard in real time. You see the full order details, payment status, delivery method, scheduled time, and any special requests — all in one place.

**Payment tracking** — Mark orders as paid or pending directly in the dashboard. No spreadsheet required. Filter by payment status to see exactly who has settled and who hasn't.

## A typical order flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer visits your menu page">
    The customer opens your branded DishInk menu link — from your WhatsApp status, Instagram bio, or any shared link. They browse your full menu with photos, descriptions, and prices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer places a structured order">
    They select items, choose quantities, add any special requests, and select their preferred delivery or pickup method. Everything is captured in a single structured form — no back-and-forth messaging.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer selects a date and time">
    For scheduled orders, the customer picks their preferred delivery or pickup date and time slot before confirming. You can configure available time slots to match your production capacity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You receive the order instantly">
    A notification arrives in your DishInk dashboard the moment the order is submitted. You see the full order: items, quantities, delivery method, scheduled time, customer details, and any special instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You prepare and update the order status">
    Work through your orders in the dashboard. Move each order from Received → Preparing → Ready → Delivered or Collected with a single tap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer receives a confirmation update">
    When you update the order status, the customer receives an automatic notification. They always know where their order is — without messaging you to ask.
  </Step>
</Steps>
