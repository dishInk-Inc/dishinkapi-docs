Fast food and self-service operations live and die by throughput. Every second a customer spends waiting at the counter, standing in a queue, or interrupting staff to ask about their order is a second that slows everyone down. DishInk is built for high-volume counter environments — fast food restaurants, food courts, takeaway operations, and collection counters — where checkout needs to be instant, kitchen coordination needs to be seamless, and customers need to know exactly when their order is ready without asking.

## The challenge: high volume, high pressure

When speed is everything, small inefficiencies multiply fast. The most common pain points for fast-food and self-service operations are:

- **Long queues** — Slow checkout creates backlogs that frustrate customers and reduce how many orders you can handle per hour.
- **No customer order visibility** — Customers can't see their order status, so they interrupt staff, crowd the counter, and slow everything down.
- **Kitchen bottlenecks** — Paper tickets pile up, orders get missed, and kitchen teams can't prioritise correctly during rushes.
- **Collection confusion** — Without a live order board, customers don't know when or where to collect — creating congestion and noise at the counter.
- **No sales or cost insight** — Without real-time reporting, you can't tell which items are selling, what your food costs look like, or how efficiently your kitchen is running.

## How DishInk solves it

DishInk connects every step from counter to collection — fast checkout, instant kitchen tickets, a live customer-facing order board, and real-time sales analytics — all from one platform.

**Fast checkout at the POS** — Your cashiers process orders quickly with a touch-optimised POS that supports modifiers, combo deals, and multiple payment methods. ZRA Smart Invoice compliance is automatic.

**Instant KDS delivery** — The moment a cashier confirms an order, it fires to the Kitchen Display System. No paper, no shouting, no lost tickets. Kitchen staff see every active order, status, and timing in real time.

**Customer-facing order board** — A KFC-style collection screen shows customers their order number moving from "Preparing" to "Ready." Customers stop crowding the counter — they watch the screen instead.

**QR receipt tracking** — Every printed receipt includes a QR code. Customers scan it on their phone to see their order status live. No app required.

**Automatic inventory deductions** — Every item sold deducts the right ingredients from stock based on your recipes. You always know what's running low before you run out mid-service.

**Real-time analytics** — See what's selling, what your food costs are, and how fast your kitchen is turning orders — all in your analytics dashboard, updated with every sale.

## A typical service flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Order placed at the POS">
    The cashier takes the order at the counter. The POS supports modifiers (e.g. "no onions"), combo items, and fast item search to keep checkout times low.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kitchen receives the ticket instantly">
    The order fires to the KDS screen in the kitchen the moment payment is confirmed. No paper ticket printed, no delay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer gets a receipt with a QR code">
    A receipt is printed or sent digitally. The QR code on the receipt links to a live order tracking page the customer can open on their phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kitchen updates order status">
    Kitchen staff mark the order as Preparing, then Ready on the KDS with a single tap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Live order board updates">
    The customer-facing collection screen reflects the status change in real time. The customer's number moves from the "Preparing" column to the "Ready" column.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer collects their order">
    No counter crowding, no asking staff for updates. The customer saw the screen, collected their order, and moved on.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key benefits

- **Shorter queues and faster throughput** — A streamlined POS checkout combined with a clear collection screen keeps the line moving and reduces average service time significantly.
- **Fewer interruptions, better kitchen focus** — Customers track orders themselves via the order board or receipt QR. Your kitchen staff focus on cooking, not answering "is my food ready?"
- **Tighter food cost and inventory control** — Every item sold automatically updates your stock levels. Know what's running low before you run out, and track your real food cost per item.
- **Real-time business performance** — Sales trends, kitchen efficiency, and food cost data are always up to date in your analytics dashboard, so you can make faster, more informed decisions every shift.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the customer-facing order board work?">
    The order board is a dedicated screen you mount at your collection counter. It connects to DishInk and updates automatically when kitchen staff change an order's status on the KDS. When an order moves from Preparing to Ready, the customer's number appears in the Ready column. No manual updates and no extra devices needed beyond the display screen itself.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can customers track orders without the order board?">
    Yes. Every receipt includes a QR code that customers scan to view live order status on their phone. The board and the QR tracking both update from the same source — the KDS status. You can offer one or both depending on your setup.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can DishInk handle large order volumes during peak hours?">
    Yes. DishInk is built for high-volume environments. The POS supports multiple simultaneous terminals on the same network, the KDS handles concurrent orders cleanly, and the LAN offline mode ensures operations continue even if the internet goes down during a rush.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does DishInk support combo meals and modifiers?">
    Yes. You can configure combo items and apply modifiers (add-ons, removals, substitutions, cooking preferences) at the POS. Modifiers fire with the order to the KDS so kitchen staff see exactly what to prepare for every ticket.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is DishInk ZRA compliant for fast-food receipts?">
    Yes. The DishInk POS is fully ZRA Smart Invoice compliant. Receipts are issued in the correct ZRA format automatically with every transaction — no separate software or manual filing required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
