Growing a restaurant group, chain, or franchise introduces a class of problems that single-location systems simply aren't built to handle. When you have two, five, or twenty branches, you need centralised visibility without sacrificing the operational independence each location requires day-to-day. DishInk's multi-location architecture lets every branch run its own POS, KDS, and staff tools independently, while your management team sees live data across all locations from a single dashboard — no manual consolidation, no switching between systems, no blind spots.

## The challenge: growth brings complexity

The systems and processes that worked for one location start to break down the moment you open a second branch. The most common problems for growing groups are:

- **Inconsistent reporting** — Each branch generates different data formats, making fair performance comparison impossible without manual work.
- **No cross-branch visibility** — Managers can only see one location at a time. Patterns and problems across the group go unnoticed until it's too late.
- **Different procedures per branch** — Without a unified system, branches develop their own methods, creating training challenges and inconsistent guest experiences.
- **Difficult performance comparisons** — Comparing sales, costs, and efficiency between branches requires manual data consolidation — slow, error-prone, and always out of date.
- **Team management at scale** — Coordinating payroll, scheduling, and HR across multiple teams in different locations without a unified system becomes a significant logistical burden.

## How DishInk solves it

DishInk provides a single platform that every branch runs on — standardising your operations, reporting, and staff management while giving each location the independence it needs on the ground.

**Centralised dashboard** — One login gives ownership and management a live view of every branch: sales, inventory, staff activity, and performance. No switching between accounts or waiting for end-of-day reports.

**Branch-level reporting** — Each location generates its own detailed reports. Drill down into any branch to see sales by hour, food cost, table turnover, or staff performance — all in the same format as every other branch.

**Branch comparison analytics** — Side-by-side performance reports show exactly which locations are thriving, which need attention, and where your biggest opportunities are across the group.

**Unified staff and payroll** — Manage staff accounts, shift schedules, attendance, and payroll across all branches from one place. No spreadsheets, no manual consolidation between branch managers.

**Inventory per branch** — Each branch tracks its own stock independently, but ownership can see aggregate inventory across all locations. Identify which branches are running efficient food costs and which are not.

**Instant branch replication** — Adding a new location takes minutes. Your menu, staff roles, tax settings, and operational configurations replicate instantly to the new branch — ready to trade from day one.

**ZRA compliance at branch level** — Every branch is fully ZRA Smart Invoice compliant automatically. No manual setup per location and no separate compliance software required.
