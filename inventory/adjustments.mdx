Inventory Adjustments correct discrepancies between physical stock and system stock. Common reasons: spillage, breakage, expiry, theft, staff meals, or count errors.

## Overview

Every adjustment records:

- Ingredient and quantity delta (positive or negative)
- Reason code (spillage, expiry, correction, staff meal, theft)
- Staff member and timestamp
- Optional note

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Log spillage and breakage the moment it happens. End-of-day batch adjustments hide operational issues.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not use adjustments to "fix" purchase records. Use [Purchase Tracking](/inventory/purchase-tracking) instead — adjustments are for actual variance.
</Warning>
