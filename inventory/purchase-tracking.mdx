Record every purchase order to keep stock levels accurate and food-cost analytics reliable.

## Overview

A purchase order (PO) captures:

- Supplier
- Line items with quantity and unit cost
- Delivery date and receipt reference
- Invoice attachment (photo or PDF)

When a PO is marked **Received**, ingredient stock rises automatically and weighted average cost updates.

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Enter POs the day of delivery, not weekly. Stock accuracy plummets when POs lag behind physical arrivals.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not mark POs as Received before physically counting the delivery. Missing items become invisible variances.
</Warning>
