Stock Levels shows the live quantity of every ingredient across every branch. Values update automatically with every sale, purchase, and adjustment.

## Overview

- Deductions happen on sale (via product recipe).
- Additions happen on purchase (see [Purchase Tracking](/inventory/purchase-tracking)).
- Manual corrections happen via [Adjustments](/inventory/adjustments).

<Frame caption="Stock levels">
  ![Stock levels](/images/inventory/stock-levels.png)

<Note>
  Stock counts sync in real time across the POS, KDS, and dashboard. Selling an out-of-stock item is blocked automatically.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Do a physical stock count at least weekly. Compare against system counts and record variances in [Adjustments](/inventory/adjustments).
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not trust system stock without a periodic physical count. Recipe errors, spillage, and staff meals cause silent drift.
</Warning>
