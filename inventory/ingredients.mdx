Ingredients are the raw materials that make up your products: flour, oil, chicken breast, whisky. DishInk tracks each ingredient's stock, cost, and usage.

## Overview

- Ingredients live in a central library shared across all products.
- Each ingredient has a unit of measure (kg, g, L, ml, unit, bottle).
- Cost per unit feeds food-cost analytics and margin reports.

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Store ingredients in the unit you buy in (per kg, per L). Convert only in the recipe (e.g. 30 g of flour per pizza). This keeps purchasing simple.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not create duplicate ingredients (e.g. "tomato" and "tomatoes"). Stock movement will scatter and reporting becomes unreliable.
</Warning>
