## Three-Layer Offline Protection

Connectivity in Zambia — and across Africa — is not always reliable. DishInk is built for this reality. Your POS and kitchen never stop, regardless of what the internet is doing.

**Layer 1 — Cloud Sync (Online)**
When you have a full internet connection, everything runs in real time. Orders, payments, inventory, and analytics sync live across all devices and your cloud dashboard.

**Layer 2 — LAN Mode (WiFi available, internet down)**
Your internet bundle drops but your router is still on. DishInk devices talk directly to each other over your local network. The POS keeps taking orders, the KDS keeps receiving tickets, and the waiter app keeps firing to the kitchen — all without a single byte of internet traffic.

**Layer 3 — Local Storage (Fully offline)**
No WiFi, no internet. Each device saves every action — orders, payments, stock deductions — to its own local storage. The moment connectivity returns, everything syncs automatically. Zero data loss.

<Tip>
  You do not need to configure any of this manually. All three layers switch automatically based on what connectivity is available at any given moment.
</Tip>

## ZRA Smart Invoice Compliance

DishInk is fully compliant with the **Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA) Smart Invoice** system out of the box. Every sale processed through the DishInk POS is recorded and receipted in the correct ZRA format. You can choose between Turnover Tax and Smart Invoice from your Settings panel — no third-party tools, no extra setup, and no manual tax calculations required. Your business stays compliant from day one.
