## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [dishink.com](https://dishink.com/auth?mode=signup) — no credit card required. Your 5-day free trial begins immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your platform">
    Add your restaurant details, upload your menu, choose a website template, and invite your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Print your QR codes, set up your POS terminal, and start taking orders. Your kitchen display and waiter app are ready to go.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grow your business">
    Use analytics to track performance, manage payroll with one tap, and expand to new locations when you're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
