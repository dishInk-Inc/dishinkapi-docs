This guide walks you through everything you need to go from zero to a live, fully operational DishInk system — from creating your account and building your menu to enabling modules, onboarding your team, and taking your very first order. Whether you're a solo café owner or a multi-location operator, you'll be up and running well before your next service.

<Info>
  **Before you begin**

  - DishInk includes a **5-day free trial** — no credit card required.
  - The platform runs entirely in the browser on any smartphone, tablet, or desktop. No app downloads or special hardware needed.
  - You can start with just a digital menu on the Starter plan and enable additional modules (POS, KDS, Inventory, Payroll, and more) as your operation grows.
</Info>

## Step-by-step setup

<Steps>

  <Step title="Create your account">
    1. Go to [https://dishink.com/auth?mode=signup](https://dishink.com/auth?mode=signup).
    2. Enter your email address and create a password.
    3. Fill in your restaurant details — name, venue type, and location.
    4. Your **5-day free trial begins immediately**. No credit card is required at this stage.

    Your dashboard is ready the moment you submit. You can start configuring your menu and modules right away.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Pick the plan that matches where your business is today — you can upgrade at any time.

    | Plan | Price | Best for |
    |---|---|---|
    | **Starter** | K399/mo | Digital menu and website for small venues (up to 15 items) |
    | **Growth** | K599/mo | Premium website, WhatsApp ordering, AI features, unlimited menu items |
    | **Operations Suite** | K4,500/mo | Full OS — POS, KDS, Inventory, Payroll, and more (2 locations included) |
    | **Enterprise** | K9,500/mo | Multi-location chains with dedicated support (up to 5 locations) |

    USD pricing is available for international businesses — see the full breakdown at [/plans-and-pricing](/plans-and-pricing). Yearly billing saves you significantly across all plans.

    <Tip>
      Not sure which plan to choose? Start with Growth for a fully featured digital presence, or contact the team to walk through the Operations Suite if you need POS and kitchen management from day one.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Build your menu">
    A well-structured menu is the foundation of everything in DishInk — it powers your digital menu, POS, KDS, and online ordering.

    1. In your dashboard, navigate to **Menu → Categories** and add your sections (e.g., Starters, Mains, Grills, Drinks, Desserts).
    2. Under each category, add your menu items. For each item you can include:
       - Name, description, and price
       - Photos (upload your own or use AI Image Import from Pexels/Pixabay)
       - Allergen notes and dietary tags
       - Modifiers and add-ons (e.g., cooking preferences, extras)
    3. Save your menu — your **QR digital menu goes live immediately**. Customers can scan and browse from any smartphone.

    **Optional (Growth and above):** Use **AI Menu Extraction** to upload a photo or PDF of your existing printed menu. The AI reads, structures, and imports your items automatically — saving hours of manual entry. You review and approve everything before it goes live.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your modules">
    DishInk is modular — enable only what you need, and add more as you grow.

    **Enable your modules**

    Go to **Settings → Modules** and toggle on the tools relevant to your venue:
    - **POS** — fast checkout for dine-in, takeaway, and counter
    - **KDS** — real-time kitchen display, replaces paper tickets
    - **Waiter App** — digital table-side ordering for front-of-house staff
    - **Tables** — live floor plan and open tab management
    - **Inventory** — recipe-based stock deductions and food cost tracking
    - **Payroll & HRM** — automated payroll, attendance, and staff management
    - **Online Ordering** — WhatsApp and digital menu ordering
    - **Analytics** — revenue, menu performance, kitchen, and staff insights

    **Add your team**

    Go to **Staff → Add Staff Member**. Create a profile for each employee, assign their role (Cashier, Waiter, Kitchen Staff, Supervisor, or Manager), and set their permissions. Each staff member gets a unique PIN for clock-in and POS access.

    **Set up payment methods**

    In **Settings → Payments**, enable the methods your venue accepts:
    - Cash
    - Card
    - MTN Mobile Money (MoMo)
    - Airtel Money
    - Split payments across multiple methods

    **Optional:** If you use a third-party reservation platform (e.g., Dineplan, OpenTable), paste your booking widget URL in **Settings → Reservations**. DishInk automatically adds a "Book Now" button to your digital menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go live">
    You're ready. Here's how to open for service:

    1. **Print your QR codes** — go to **QR Codes** in your dashboard, download each table's code as a PNG, and print them on table tents or stickers. Laminate for durability.
    2. **Open POS** on your cashier terminal or device and confirm it's connected to your network.
    3. **Staff clock in** — each team member enters their PIN on any OS terminal to start their shift.
    4. **Take your first order** — via POS, Waiter App, or directly from a customer scanning a table QR code.

    Orders fire to the kitchen instantly. Table status updates in real time. You're live.

    <Tip>
      If your internet drops during service, DishInk keeps running. LAN Mode keeps your POS and KDS communicating over your local network, and Offline Mode saves every action locally — syncing automatically when connectivity is restored.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

</Steps>

## Signing in

**Managers and owners** sign in with their email address and password at [https://dishink.com/auth](https://dishink.com/auth).

**Staff members** use PIN-based login. On the same login page, switch to **Staff PIN Login** and enter your assigned PIN to access the POS, Waiter App, or clock-in terminal.

<Tip>
  Staff PINs are created and managed by managers in the **Staff Management** section of the dashboard. Each staff member gets a unique PIN tied to their role and permissions.
</Tip>
