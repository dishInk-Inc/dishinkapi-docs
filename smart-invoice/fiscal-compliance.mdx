DishInk keeps you compliant with ZRA regulations automatically. This page summarises the compliance features and audit-ready outputs available.

## Overview

Compliance features:

- Every sale submitted to ZRA in real time (VAT mode)
- Verified QR code on every receipt
- Full audit trail: order, edits, refunds, voids, timestamps, staff
- Immutable invoice records (deletions blocked)
- Batch invoice generation for reporting periods
- Configurable tax rules for special-status items

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Reconcile your Smart Invoice batch to your bank deposits monthly. Discrepancies are easier to explain when the trail is fresh.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not manually edit any exported CSV before submitting to ZRA. Edits break the audit hash and can trigger investigation.
</Warning>
