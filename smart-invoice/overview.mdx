## Overview

- Real-time submission to ZRA on every sale (VAT mode)
- Verified ZRA QR code printed on every receipt
- VAT-compliant line-item breakdowns
- Full audit trail for regulatory review
- Batch invoice generation for reporting periods
- Turnover tax mode for non-VAT SMEs

## Two tax modes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Turnover Tax (SME)">
    Designed for smaller businesses that are not VAT-registered. Tax is calculated as a percentage of total sales turnover.

    - Automatically calculated on every sale
    - Receipt shows Turnover Tax (TOT) breakdown
    - ZRA-compliant receipt format
    - No VAT registration required
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="ZRA Smart Invoice (VAT)">
    Full ZRA Smart Invoice integration for VAT-registered businesses. Every sale is submitted in real time and returns a verified QR code.

    - Real-time submission on every sale
    - Verified ZRA QR code on every receipt
    - VAT-compliant line-item breakdowns
    - Audit-ready transaction logs
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Switch modes under **Settings → Billing and Tax**.

<Note>
  ZRA integration requires your taxpayer identification and VAT number configured under Settings. See [ZRA Integration](/smart-invoice/zra-integration).
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Test the integration on a low-value sale before your first busy service. Confirms the connection is live and receipts print with the QR code.
</Tip>
