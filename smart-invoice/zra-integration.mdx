Connect your DishInk account to the ZRA Smart Invoice system. One-time setup takes about 10 minutes.

## Overview

Requirements:

- ZRA taxpayer identification number (TPIN)
- VAT registration certificate (for VAT mode)
- ZRA Smart Invoice API credentials (issued by ZRA)

## Connect ZRA

<Frame caption="ZRA integration">
  ![ZRA setup](/images/smart-invoice/zra-integration.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  If the connection to ZRA drops temporarily, DishInk queues invoices locally and submits them once connectivity is restored. Receipts still print with the correct data.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Keep your VAT registration certificate scanned and uploaded to Settings → Documents for quick audit response.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not share Smart Invoice API credentials. Treat them like a bank password — rotate if a staff member with access leaves.
</Warning>
