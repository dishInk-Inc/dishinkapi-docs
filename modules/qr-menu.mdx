Every DishInk account comes with a hosted restaurant website and a dynamic QR menu — both live at your unique link the moment you save your first menu item. Customers scan a QR code with any phone camera, and your full menu opens instantly in their browser. No app download, no login, no friction. When you update a price, mark an item sold out, or add a special, every scan reflects the change immediately — your printed QR codes never need reprinting.

## Scan & Browse

Your digital menu is optimised for the way real customers behave in a restaurant: quick scan, instant open, browse by photo.

- **No app required** — any phone camera opens the menu directly in the browser
- **No login required** — zero sign-up friction for your guests
- **Per-table QR codes** — each table has a unique code so the system knows which table is scanning
- **Mobile-optimised** — built for small screens with fast load times, even on slow connections
- **Photo-rich display** — every item can show a high-quality photo, description, and allergen notes
- **Always current** — changes go live the moment you hit save in your dashboard

## Live Menu Management

Manage your menu from any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. Changes publish the instant you save, with no developers or reprinting involved.

- **Live price updates** — change any price and it reflects on the next customer scan
- **Sold-out toggle** — mark items unavailable in one tap; they grey out on the customer menu automatically
- **Add specials** — publish seasonal or daily specials instantly without a menu reprint
- **Category management** — create, reorder, and rename menu categories at any time
- **Photo uploads** — add images directly from your phone or desktop; images are auto-optimised for mobile
- **Allergen notes** — attach allergen and dietary information per item
- **Item descriptions** — add preparation details, ingredients, or origin stories to any dish

<Tip>
Use the AI Menu Extraction feature (Growth+ plans) to photograph your existing paper menu and import all items automatically — no manual data entry required.
</Tip>

## Full Website Included

Your DishInk account is not just a menu — it is a complete hosted restaurant website available at `dishink.com/r/your-restaurant`, secured with SSL, with zero hosting fees.

Every website includes the following pages out of the box:

| Page | What It Contains |
|---|---|
| **Homepage** | Hero image, restaurant name, tagline, and call-to-action buttons |
| **Photo Gallery** | Upload food and atmosphere photos from any device; auto-optimised and live instantly |
| **About** | Your story, opening hours, contact details, and social media links |
| **Map & Directions** | Add your location once; guests get one-tap directions via Google or Apple Maps |
| **Digital Menu** | Your full QR menu embedded in the website so customers can browse before they visit |
| **Reservations & Feedback** | Built-in booking request form and guest feedback capture, direct to your dashboard |

Share your link on WhatsApp, social media, Google Business, or printed flyers. Customers can view your full website and menu on any device, even without a QR code to scan.

<Info>
**Menu-Only Mode:** If you already have a website and want to use DishInk solely as a digital menu, enable Menu-Only Mode in your settings. Your DishInk link goes straight to the menu page — no full website displayed. Add a 'View Menu' button on your existing site that links to your DishInk menu URL.
</Info>

## QR Code Designer

Design a branded QR code that looks as good as your restaurant, entirely within your DishInk dashboard — no third-party tools needed.

- **Custom colours** — match the QR code fill and background to your brand palette
- **Logo in centre** — drop your restaurant logo into the centre of the code
- **Print-ready download** — export a high-resolution PNG ready for table tents, stickers, or display stands
- **Per-table codes** — generate a unique code for every table; each one links back to the same dynamic menu

<Note>
DishInk uses dynamic QR codes. The code always points to the same permanent URL. You can change your menu, rebrand, or switch templates — your existing printed codes still work. You only ever need to reprint if a physical code is damaged or lost.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

    No. Customers scan with their standard phone camera — no app download, no login, no account creation. The menu opens directly in their mobile browser in seconds.

  <Accordion title="Do QR codes need to be reprinted when I update the menu?">
    No. DishInk uses dynamic QR codes that always point to the same permanent link. Update prices, add specials, or change templates — your existing printed codes continue to work. Reprint only if a physical code is damaged or lost.
  </Accordion>

    Starter plans include up to 15 menu items. Growth, Operations Suite, and Enterprise plans include unlimited menu items across unlimited categories.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use DishInk just as a digital menu without a full website?">
    Yes. Enable Menu-Only Mode in your settings and your DishInk link goes straight to the menu page. Add a 'View Menu' button on your existing website that links to your DishInk menu URL — no migration required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my own booking platform for reservations?">
    Yes. Paste your booking URL from any platform — Dineplan, OpenTable, SevenRooms, Resy, or any other service that provides a direct link — into Settings. A Book Now button appears automatically on your menu. No code or API key needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
