DishInk WhatsApp Ordering lets your customers place structured delivery and takeaway orders entirely through WhatsApp — the app already on every phone in Africa. Customers browse your photo menu, build a cart with modifiers and delivery details, and tap once to send a pre-filled order message straight to your team. No app download, no account creation, no checkout platform to manage. It is built for restaurants, cafes, and home kitchens that want to take online orders without the complexity or commission fees of a third-party delivery marketplace.

<Note>
WhatsApp Ordering is available on the **Growth plan and above**. Enable it in Settings → Features → WhatsApp Ordering.
</Note>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer Scans & Browses">
    The customer scans your restaurant QR code or visits your menu link. They browse your full menu with item photos, prices, descriptions, and modifiers — no app, no login, any device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer Builds Cart">
    The customer adds items, selects modifiers, adjusts quantities, adds per-item notes, schedules a delivery date and time, and provides their delivery location — all within the cart before WhatsApp is opened.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer Pays">
    The customer copies your payment number (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Zamtel, or bank), sends the payment directly to you, and confirms in the cart. DishInk does **not** process payments — you receive 100% of every sale directly from the customer, with no platform commission deducted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer Sends Order via WhatsApp">
    The customer taps **Send Order**. WhatsApp opens with a fully pre-filled message containing the complete order — items, quantities, modifiers, total, name, delivery location, and scheduled time. One tap sends it to your restaurant's WhatsApp number instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Order Management Dashboard

Every WhatsApp order appears in your DishInk Orders dashboard automatically alongside the WhatsApp message. You manage the entire order lifecycle from one screen.

- **Live order feed** — all incoming orders appear in real time, sorted by status
- **Status updates** — move each order through Pending → Preparing → Dispatched → Delivered → Cancelled
- **Payment tracking** — mark orders as paid or unpaid; log payment confirmation
- **Filter & search** — filter by date range, customer name, or order status
- **Export reports** — download order data for accounting or tax in one click
- **Customer reviews** — log and track guest feedback per order

## Delivery Modes

DishInk integrates with Yango Delivery and gives you two modes to match how your business operates. Switch between them at any time in **Settings → WhatsApp Ordering**.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="We Handle Delivery">
    **Your team creates the Yango order**

    The customer provides their delivery location in the cart at checkout. A map pin is included in the WhatsApp order message. Once you confirm payment, you create the Yango delivery request on your end. The customer pays the driver directly on arrival.

    - Location collection step shown in the customer cart
    - Map pin included in the WhatsApp message
    - You create and manage the Yango delivery
    - Customer pays the driver directly
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Customer Handles Delivery">
    **Customer arranges their own Yango order**

    The location step is skipped in the customer cart, simplifying the checkout flow. After you confirm the order is ready, the customer places their own Yango delivery request. Ideal for restaurants that want to focus on preparation and packaging only.

    - Location step removed from the cart
    - Simpler, faster customer checkout
    - Customer places their own Yango order after confirmation
    - You prepare and package the order only
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Scheduled Orders

Customers can schedule deliveries for any future date and time directly in the cart — no extra tools or third-party scheduling needed.

- **Date picker** — customer selects any available date from the cart
- **Time slot selector** — customer picks a preferred delivery window
- **Automatic inclusion** — the scheduled date and time are included in the pre-filled WhatsApp message automatically
- **ASAP option** — customers can also request immediate delivery without specifying a slot

Your team sees the scheduled time in both the WhatsApp message and the Orders dashboard, so you know exactly when to have each order ready.

## Custom Message Template

Customise the pre-filled WhatsApp message that customers send to match your brand voice. Configure your template in **Settings → Features → WhatsApp Order Message** using the following tokens:

| Token | Replaced With |
|---|---|
| `{restaurant}` | Your restaurant name |
| `{items}` | Full itemised order list with quantities and modifiers |
| `{total}` | Order total in Kwacha |
| `{name}` | Customer's name |
| `{date}` | Scheduled delivery date |
| `{time}` | Scheduled delivery time |
| `{location}` | Customer's delivery location or map pin |

Leave the template blank to use the smart default format. Every order sent uses your saved template going forward.

## Go Live in Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the Toggle">
    Go to **Settings → Features → WhatsApp Ordering** and toggle it on. An Order via WhatsApp button appears on your menu immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your WhatsApp Number">
    Enter the WhatsApp number where orders should be sent. This is the number customers' pre-filled messages will be directed to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Payment Instructions">
    Add your payment details — MTN MoMo number, Airtel Money number, Zamtel number, or bank account — with a clear label for customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Delivery Mode">
    Select **We Handle** or **Customer Handles** under delivery settings. The cart flow updates instantly to match your choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Share your menu link, post your QR code, and start receiving orders. Every new order lands in your Orders dashboard automatically alongside the WhatsApp message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do customers need to download an app to place an order?">
    No. Customers browse your menu in their phone browser and the order is sent through WhatsApp, which is already installed on virtually every phone. Nothing else to install.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does DishInk process payments?">
    No. DishInk does not handle or process any payments. Customers pay directly to your mobile money or bank account number, which you configure in Settings. You keep 100% of every sale — there are no platform commissions or transaction fees deducted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can customers schedule deliveries for a later time or date?">
    Yes. The cart includes a date picker and time slot selector. The customer's chosen delivery time is included automatically in the pre-filled WhatsApp message and visible in your Orders dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I choose the delivery mode?">
    Go to **Settings → WhatsApp Ordering** and select either **We Handle** (you create the Yango order) or **Customer Handles** (the customer places their own Yango order after confirmation). The customer's cart flow updates instantly to match whichever mode you select. You can switch at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What plan do I need for WhatsApp Ordering?">
    WhatsApp Ordering is available on the **Growth plan and above** — that includes Growth, Operations Suite, and Enterprise. If you are on Starter, upgrade to Growth at any time from your dashboard to unlock this feature.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
