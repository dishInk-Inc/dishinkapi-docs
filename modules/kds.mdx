DishInk KDS replaces paper kitchen tickets with live digital screens that your kitchen team can read, act on, and update in real time. The moment an order is placed on the POS or Waiter App it fires to the KDS — no printing, no shouting, no lost tickets. Every status change syncs instantly across the KDS, POS, Waiter App, and Table Management dashboard, so your front-of-house and kitchen are always reading from the same page.

## Hall View vs Station View

DishInk KDS offers two display modes so you can match the screen to the role.

**Hall View** gives a full overview of every active order across the entire kitchen. It is designed for head chefs, kitchen managers, and supervisors who need to see the complete picture at a glance — workload, timing, and priority all on one screen. Colour-coded status cards and time indicators make it easy to spot what needs attention without walking the floor.

**Station View** filters the display to show only the items relevant to a specific kitchen section. Set up stations to match your kitchen layout:

- Grill
- Fry
- Bar
- Prep
- Any custom station you create

Each cook sees only their items, which reduces screen clutter, speeds up preparation, and eliminates the confusion of reading a full-kitchen ticket at a single station.

## Order Lifecycle

Every order moves through a clear four-stage lifecycle. Status updates sync in real time across every connected screen and app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New">
    Order placed on POS or Waiter App. Ticket appears on KDS instantly with all items, modifiers, and notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preparing">
    Kitchen taps to confirm. Timer starts. Status updates across KDS, POS, Waiter App, and Table Management simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ready">
    Kitchen marks the order ready. Waiter is notified. The ticket moves to the Ready column on the customer order board.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed">
    Order collected and closed. Ticket clears from the active screen and is logged in order history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prep Time Tracking

Every ticket carries a live timer from the moment it enters the queue. DishInk uses colour to communicate urgency at a glance so your team never needs to calculate elapsed time manually.

| Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 Green | Fresh ticket — within expected prep time |
| 🟡 Amber | Approaching the target time — needs attention |
| 🔴 Red | Overdue — action required immediately |

You control when the timer starts: configure it to begin the moment an order is sent, or only when a kitchen team member taps **Preparing**. Station-level timing lets you track performance per section independently.

## Customer Order Board

Display a KFC-style customer-facing order board on any screen in your venue. Customers see their order number move from **Preparing** to **Ready** — no need to ask staff, queue at the counter, or wait for a shout-out.

- Order numbers update automatically in real time
- Full-screen mode optimised for smart TVs and mounted tablets
- Light and dark mode for different venue environments
- Brand-customisable to match your restaurant colours and name
- Add a photo gallery carousel to the display during quiet periods
- Reduces counter congestion and staff interruptions during peak service

Run the customer order board on any Android TV stick or tablet — no dedicated hardware required.

## QR Order Tracking

Every receipt printed from DishInk includes a unique QR code. Customers scan it with their phone camera to open a live order-tracking page — no app download, no login required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Order Placed">
    Receipt prints with a unique tracking QR code alongside the ZRA Smart Invoice QR (if applicable).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer Scans">
    Customer opens the live tracking page on their phone in one tap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kitchen Updates">
    Every status change — Preparing, Ready — reflects instantly on the customer's screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ready to Collect">
    Customer sees **Ready**, heads to the counter. No pestering staff, no shouting order numbers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
