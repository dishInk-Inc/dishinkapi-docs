DishInk POS is a touch-based point-of-sale system built for restaurants, bars, cafes, and fast-food venues across Africa. Run it on any Android phone, tablet, touchscreen monitor, or browser — no specialist hardware required. It handles every order scenario from seated dine-in to drive-through takeaway, connects directly to the Kitchen Display and Waiter App in real time, and keeps you compliant with Zambia Revenue Authority requirements out of the box.

## Order Management

Create orders instantly from any device — POS terminal, iPad, desktop, tablet, or handheld phone. Every order type runs through the same clean interface so your team never has to switch tools.

- **Fast order creation** — browse your menu by category, tap to add, and send to the kitchen in seconds
- **Dine-in, takeaway & delivery** — one interface covers every scenario
- **Menu item modifiers** — add extras, remove ingredients, and set cooking preferences per item
- **Order notes** — attach special instructions to individual items or entire orders
- **Open tabs** — keep an order live across multiple rounds before billing
- **Order editing** — adjust quantities, swap items, or void lines before the bill is closed
- **Quick reorder** — refire a previous order with a single tap, ideal for regulars

## Split Billing

Handle large group payments without holding up service. DishInk gives you three split modes so every guest pays exactly what they owe.

| Split Mode | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Split equally** | Divide the total by the number of people |
| **Split by item** | Each person pays for the specific items they ordered |
| **Custom amount** | Enter any amount per person for flexible arrangements |

Each person in the split can pay with a different payment method and receive their own individual receipt or share a combined receipt — your cashier controls the preference per transaction.

## Receipts

Every receipt is handled automatically from the moment an order is placed through to final payment and reprints.

- **Thermal printing** — one-tap print to any Bluetooth, Network, or QZ Tray compatible receipt printer
- **QR code on receipt** — customers scan to track their order in real time from preparing through to ready
- **Provisional bills** — issue a pre-bill before final payment, ideal for quick-service counters
- **Reprint anytime** — pull up any receipt from order history and reprint or share it instantly

## ZRA Smart Invoice

DishInk is integrated with the Zambia Revenue Authority Smart Invoice system. Select your tax method once in Settings and every receipt updates automatically.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Turnover Tax (SME)">
    Designed for smaller businesses that are not VAT-registered. Tax is calculated as a percentage of total sales turnover.

    - Automatically calculated on every sale
    - Receipt shows Turnover Tax (TOT) breakdown
    - ZRA-compliant receipt format
    - No VAT registration required
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="ZRA Smart Invoice (VAT)">
    Full ZRA Smart Invoice integration for VAT-registered businesses. Every sale is submitted to ZRA in real time and returns a verified QR code.

    - Real-time submission to ZRA on every sale
    - Verified ZRA QR code printed on every receipt
    - VAT-compliant line-item breakdowns
    - Audit-ready transaction logs
    - Works across all DishInk receipt types
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Switch between modes at any time under **Settings → Billing and Tax**. All receipts update instantly.

## Multi-Terminal & Branches

Scale from a single counter to a multi-branch operation without changing how your team works.

- **Unlimited branches** — manage every location from one account
- **Unlimited terminals** — run a bar terminal, cashier POS, and server handheld simultaneously, all in sync
- **Manager PIN control** — restrict sensitive actions behind a manager PIN
- **Order transfers** — manager enters PIN and moves any open order to another terminal instantly
- **All-terminal view** — see every live transaction across the floor in one screen
- **Order history** — full audit trail of every transaction across all terminals and branches

<Note>
DishInk POS has three layers of offline protection: **Cloud → LAN → Local Storage**. If your internet drops, devices communicate over your local WiFi. If the local network drops, orders save to device storage. Everything syncs back to the cloud automatically when connectivity is restored — service never stops.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I split bills between multiple customers?">
    Yes. Split equally, by item, or by a custom amount. Each person can pay with a different method — cash, card, or mobile money — and receive their own receipt or share a combined one. The cashier controls the split type at the point of payment.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does DishInk process card payments?">
    No. DishInk records transactions alongside your own payment providers and card terminals. You keep full control of your money flow — DishInk never touches your funds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does the POS work offline?">
    Yes. DishInk creates a backup LAN connection so your devices communicate over your local WiFi even without internet. If the network also drops, orders save to local device storage. Once connectivity is restored, everything syncs to the cloud automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I run multiple POS terminals at the same time?">
    Yes. You can run unlimited terminals simultaneously — bar counter, takeaway window, and dining floor all at once. Every terminal stays fully in sync in real time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the POS ZRA Smart Invoice compliant?">
    Yes. DishInk is integrated with the Zambia Revenue Authority Smart Invoice system. Choose between Turnover Tax and Smart Invoice in Settings — receipts update automatically to match your selection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I generate batch invoices for reporting?">
    Yes. DishInk lets you consolidate all transactions for a given day or any custom date range into a single batch invoice, making end-of-period reporting and tax submissions straightforward.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
