DishInk Table Management gives you a live digital floor plan of your entire venue so you always know what is happening at every table. Every status change, payment event, and order update reflects across the POS, KDS, and Waiter App the moment it happens. It is built for full-service restaurants, bars, hotel dining rooms, and any venue that needs to track table occupancy, manage service flow, and prevent orders from falling through the cracks.

## Live Floor Dashboard

Your entire floor becomes a single, always-current screen. Each table displays as a live card showing everything your team needs at a glance.

- **Status colour** — instant visual read on where every table is in the service lifecycle
- **Guest count & duration** — number of guests and time elapsed since seating
- **Running bill total** — live running total updates as items are added
- **Waiter badge** — name of the assigned server on every card
- **Split billing indicator** — purple border shows tables in an active split
- **Overdue alert** — pulsing red border for tables that have been waiting too long
- **Section organisation** — create sections (Indoor, Outdoor, Bar, VIP) and add tables freely
- **Real-time updates** — every event syncs across POS, KDS, and Waiter App simultaneously

## Table Status Colours

Eight statuses cover every stage of the guest journey. The colour system gives managers an instant floor read without opening a single order.

| Status | Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **Available** | Default / neutral | Table is clean and ready for seating |
| **Occupied** | Solid fill | Guests seated, no order placed yet |
| **Ordering** | Active colour | Waiter is building or has sent the order |
| **Served** | Confirmed fill | Food delivered, guests eating |
| **Billing** | Billing accent | Guests ready to pay, bill generated |
| **Reserved** | Reserved fill | Table held for an upcoming booking |
| **Overdue** | Pulsing red border | Table has exceeded the expected service time |
| **Split Bill** | Purple border | Active split billing in progress |

Status colours update in real time — no refreshing, no manual changes.

## Waiter Assignment

Assign any table to any waiter directly from the floor plan. Every table card shows a waiter badge so coverage gaps are visible at a glance.

- Assign tables at seating or reassign mid-service without disruption
- Waiters see only their assigned tables by default in the Waiter App
- Managers can view all tables with full assignment overview
- Track floor coverage across sections to balance workload during peak service

Reassigning a table does not affect the open order or running bill — the new waiter picks up exactly where the previous one left off.

## Open Tabs & Running Bills

Keep a table's bill open across the entire visit. Guests can order multiple rounds without the bill being closed between them.

- Add items to an open tab at any point during the visit
- Running totals update automatically with every new round
- Full order history per table showing every item ordered and when
- N-way split billing — split equally or by specific items across any number of guests
- Per-table session summaries for end-of-service reconciliation

The open tab model is ideal for bars, lounges, and restaurants with long dining occasions where guests order across multiple courses or drink rounds.

## QR Digital Menu

Every table in DishInk has a unique QR code guests can scan to browse your full digital menu on their own phone.

- Table-specific codes let the system identify which table is scanning
- Photo-rich menu with categories, descriptions, and allergen information
- No app download required — any phone camera and browser
- Always up to date — changes made in your dashboard reflect instantly on every scan
- Replaces printed paper menus entirely, saving ongoing reprint costs

## Table Ordering Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Guest Seated">
    Waiter marks the table as occupied. Status updates to Occupied across all screens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="QR Menu Browsed">
    Guest scans the table QR code and browses the digital menu with photos on their phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Waiter Takes Order">
    Waiter builds the cart in the Waiter App with modifiers and notes, or takes the order at the POS terminal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="KDS Receives Instantly">
    Order fires to the Kitchen Display the moment the waiter taps send — no paper tickets, no verbal relay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Food Served">
    Kitchen marks items ready on the KDS. Waiter collects and delivers. Table status moves to Served.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bill Generated">
    Waiter requests the bill from the floor. Running total is finalised. Split billing initiated if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment Taken">
    Cashier processes payment on the POS — cash, card, mobile money, or split across methods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Table Released">
    Bill closed, table cleaned, status returns to Available and is ready for the next seating.
  </Step>
</Steps>
