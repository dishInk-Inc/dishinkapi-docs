DishInk uses a single consistent shell across every module. The sidebar and top bar stay fixed while the main content area changes as you move between sections.

## Top bar

The top bar runs across the full width of the screen on desktop and contains:

| Element | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Sidebar toggle** | Show or hide the left sidebar |
| **Front ops / Back ops switcher** | Jump between your public-facing tools and your restaurant operations |
| **Staff badge** | Shows the currently logged-in staff member with a quick logout / switch account button |
| **Location switcher** | Appears only for multi-location accounts — switch between branches without logging out |
| **Page title** | Displays the current module name (e.g. `DISHINK POINT OF SALE`) |

## Left sidebar

The sidebar is divided into two sections that you can collapse or expand independently.

### Front Operations

Tools for managing your public-facing restaurant presence:

- Front Ops Dashboard
- Manage Menu
- Add Menu Item
- Website Content
- Website Gallery
- Bookings & Messages
- Online Orders
- Events Management
- QR Codes

### Back Operations

Restaurant operations tools (requires Growth Pro):

- Operations Dashboard
- Kitchen Display System
- Table Management
- Waiter Interface
- Point of Sale
- Inventory Management
- Staff Management
- HRM & Payroll Suite
- Business Analytics (with Kitchen, Revenue, Menu, and Staff sub-views)
- LAN Monitor

<Note>
  Back Operations items are locked behind the Growth Pro plan. Locked items appear greyed out with a **Growth Pro \+** label. Individual sub-items within Analytics can also be restricted per staff permission.
</Note>

### Collapsing the sidebar

Click the **chevron button** on the right edge of the sidebar to collapse it to icon-only mode. Click the **panel icon** in the top bar to hide it entirely. Both states are remembered across sessions.

## Front ops vs Back ops

The **Front ops / Back ops** switcher in the top bar controls which section appears at the top of your sidebar. Set it to whichever you use most — DishInk remembers your preference.

| Front ops | Back ops |
| --- | --- |
| Menu, website, online orders, QR codes | POS, KDS, waiter, tables, inventory, payroll |
| Customer-facing tools | Restaurant operations tools |

## Bottom of sidebar

The bottom strip contains your account controls:

| Control | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Staff badge** | Your name and role while logged in as a staff member |
| **Settings** | Front Ops Settings and Back Ops Settings (dropdown) |
| **Clock In / My Earnings** | Visible to staff only — attendance and pay tracker |
| **View Site** | Opens your public restaurant page in a new tab |
| **Theme toggle** | Cycles between Light, Dark, and System theme |
| **Logout** | Signs out and returns to the login screen |

## Mobile navigation

On mobile, the sidebar is hidden by default behind a **hamburger menu button** in the top-left corner. Tap it to open the full sidebar as a slide-in drawer. Tap outside it or the × button to close.

<Warning>
  On tablets in portrait mode the sidebar is hidden by default. If a feature appears missing, open the sidebar first before assuming it isn't available.
</Warning>

## Offline mode

DishInk works offline for core operations. When you lose internet, the following modules stay fully functional:

- Point of Sale
- Waiter Interface
- Kitchen Display System
- Table Management
- LAN Monitor
- Inventory (read-only)

All other pages (reports, settings, payroll, etc.) require an active connection and will show an **Internet required** screen with quick links back to the offline-capable modules.

## Staff permissions and role restrictions

What appears in your sidebar depends on your role:

- **Owners and Managers** see everything
- **Other staff roles** (Waiter, Cashier, Kitchen) only see items their permissions allow
- Items you don't have access to are hidden entirely — not just greyed out
