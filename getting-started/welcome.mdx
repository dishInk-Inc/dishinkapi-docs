DishInk Hospitality Suite is an all-in-one restaurant operating system that connects your point of sale, kitchen, tables, waiters, inventory, staff, payroll, online ordering, and reporting into a single platform. This documentation is written for restaurant owners, managers, cashiers, waiters, kitchen staff, and administrators.
