Every DishInk shift begins with a **clock in** and ends with a **clock out**. Clock-in captures the opening cash float; clock-out generates a shift report and reconciles the cash drawer.

## Overview

- Clock-in starts payroll time tracking and records the opening float.
- Clock-out closes the shift, generates a shift report, and prompts cash reconciliation.
- Every action on the terminal between clock-in and clock-out is linked to the staff member.

<Tip>
  Count the drawer twice at both clock-in and clock-out. A two-person count during handovers eliminates most disputes.
</Tip>
