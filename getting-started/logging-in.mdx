DishInk supports two authentication methods: full email \+ password login for managers and administrators, and short PIN login for cashiers, waiters, and kitchen staff on shared terminals.

## Overview

- **Email \+ password** — for owners, administrators, and general managers on the dashboard.
- **PIN login** — for cashiers, waiters, and kitchen staff on shared POS terminals, tablets, and KDS screens.
- **SSO (enterprise)** — Google Workspace and Microsoft 365 single sign-on for larger deployments.

## Two-factor authentication

Administrators and owners can enable 2FA under **Settings → Security**. Once enabled, an authenticator-app code is required after email \+ password.
