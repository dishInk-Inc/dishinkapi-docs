DishInk HRM & Payroll covers everything between your staff clocking in and receiving their pay slip. Owners and managers configure wage structures, deduction packages, and tip distribution rules once — then generate payroll for the entire team with a single tap. Shifts, tips, advances, and deductions all pull in automatically. No spreadsheets, no manual calculations, no missed line items.

## Automated Payroll Generation

Select a pay period, choose bulk (all staff) or single staff, and tap Generate. DishInk pulls every completed shift, all tips recorded at POS, active deductions, and outstanding salary advances — then calculates each staff member's pay in seconds.

**Payroll lifecycle — every record moves through four stages:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Draft">
    Payroll is generated and calculations are complete. Review line items before approving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approved">
    Manager has reviewed and approved the record. Ready for payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paid">
    Payment confirmed. PDF pay slip generated and available for the staff member to download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disputed">
    Flagged for review. Manager and staff member resolve any discrepancy before re-approving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can run payroll for the entire team simultaneously with bulk generation, or generate a single staff member's payroll independently — useful for mid-period adjustments or leavers.

## Pay Slip Breakdown

Every pay slip is fully itemised so staff see exactly how their total was calculated. There are no hidden line items and no room for disputes over the numbers.

| Line item | Description |
|---|---|
| **Base earnings** | Hours or days worked × rate, or fixed monthly salary |
| **Overtime pay** | Hours beyond your OT threshold × the configured multiplier |
| **Direct tips** | Tips linked to this staff member's POS orders (To Waiter mode) |
| **Pool tip share** | Share of pooled tips, weighted by hours worked (Equal Split mode) |
| **Commission** | Percentage of sales, if configured in wage structure |
| **Bonuses** | Any manually added bonuses for the period |
| **Deductions** | PAYE, NAPSA, custom fixed/percentage deductions, advance repayments |
| **Net pay** | Take-home total after all deductions |

PDF pay slips are generated automatically at the end of each pay period. Staff can view and download them from their personal dashboard at any time.

**Overtime rules** — set a daily hours threshold (e.g. 8 hours) and an overtime multiplier of 1.5× or 2×. Every hour beyond the threshold is paid at the multiplier rate automatically. Pay schedules (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) are configured per restaurant in Payroll Settings.

## Tip Distribution

All tips collected through the POS flow into payroll automatically. You choose the distribution mode that fits your team culture — the system handles the maths.

**Equal Split (Pool Mode)**

All tips from the period are pooled and distributed among active staff, weighted by hours worked. Cash, card, and mobile money tips are all included in the pool.

- Every active staff member shares the pool proportionally
- Hours-weighted calculation — longer shifts receive a larger share
- No manual tip counting or end-of-night disputes

**To Waiter Mode**

Tips from POS orders are linked directly to the waiter assigned to that order. Unassigned tips (e.g. tips on cash orders with no assigned waiter) are automatically added to the pool and redistributed.

- Direct tip ownership per waiter — your best servers earn more
- Full tip-type breakdown on each pay slip: cash, card, pool share
- Unassigned tips handled automatically

<Info>
  Switch between tip distribution modes in **Settings → Payroll → Tip Distribution**. The change applies from the next payroll period.
</Info>

## Salary Advances

Staff can request a salary advance through their personal dashboard. The request includes a reason and the amount needed. Managers receive an instant notification and approve or decline with one tap. Approved advances are automatically deducted from the staff member's next pay slip — no manual tracking required.

**Advance workflow:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Staff submits request">
    The staff member opens their dashboard, enters the requested amount and a reason, and submits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manager approves or declines">
    The manager receives a notification and approves or declines. They can add a note for the record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Advance is paid out">
    The approved amount is paid to the staff member outside of the system (cash or transfer).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-deducted on next pay slip">
    The advance amount appears as a deduction line item on the next payroll run — automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Advance limits** — set a maximum per single advance request and a monthly cap per staff member to keep advances manageable across the team.

## Deductions Management

Create deductions as fixed amounts or percentages and assign them to individual staff members. Assigned deductions apply automatically on every payroll run — no manual entry each period.

DishInk includes preset deduction packages for Zambian businesses:

| Package | Deductions included |
|---|---|
| **Zambia Standard** | PAYE 25% + NAPSA 5% |
| **Basic** | PAYE 25% only |
| **Full Package** | PAYE 25% + NAPSA 5% + Medical K200 + Uniform K50 |
| **Hospitality** | PAYE 25% + Staff Meals K150 + Uniform K75 |

You can also build custom deduction packages with any combination of fixed amounts or percentages — useful for uniform fees, staff meal contributions, or any other agreed deduction.



## Payroll Reports

The Reports tab gives you a full view of every payroll period with KPI cards, composition breakdowns, and a detailed sortable table.

**What's included:**

- KPI cards: total staff paid, total gross pay, total net pay, total hours, overtime hours
- Payroll composition chart: base vs tips vs deductions vs net
- Net pay by staff member — sortable by name, amount, or hours
- Hours breakdown per staff member with overtime highlighted
- Full detailed table: every staff member's gross, net, hours, OT, tips, deductions, and status
- **CSV export** — one tap exports all payroll data for any period, ready for accounting or tax submissions

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is payroll calculated?">
    DishInk pulls data from completed shifts (hours worked), tips recorded at the POS, active deductions assigned to the staff member, and any outstanding salary advances. It calculates base pay using the configured wage structure, adds overtime, tips, commission, and bonuses, then deducts PAYE, NAPSA, advance repayments, and any custom deductions to produce the net pay.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What wage types are supported?">
    DishInk supports hourly, daily, and fixed monthly salary structures. You can also set an overtime multiplier (1.5× or 2×) with a configurable daily hour threshold, and a commission percentage based on revenue served — all configured per staff member from the Wages tab.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does tip distribution work?">
    You choose the mode in Settings. **Equal Split** pools all tips and divides them among active staff, weighted by hours worked. **To Waiter** links tips directly from POS orders to the assigned waiter — unassigned tips go to the pool. Both modes calculate automatically and appear as itemised lines on each pay slip.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can staff request salary advances?">
    Yes. Staff submit a request through their personal dashboard with a reason and amount. The manager approves or declines with one tap. Approved advances are recorded and automatically deducted from the next payroll run — no manual tracking needed. You can set per-request and monthly cap limits per staff member.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What deductions are pre-configured?">
    DishInk includes four Zambian preset packages: Zambia Standard (PAYE + NAPSA), Basic (PAYE only), Full Package (PAYE + NAPSA + Medical + Uniform), and Hospitality (PAYE + Staff Meals + Uniform). You can also create custom deductions as fixed amounts or percentages for any other payroll deduction your business uses.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I export payroll data?">
    Yes. The Reports tab includes a CSV export button for every pay period. The export includes all columns: gross pay, net pay, hours, overtime, tips, deductions by type, and status — ready for your accountant or tax submissions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
