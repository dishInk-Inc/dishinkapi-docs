DishInk Staff Management gives you a single dashboard for everything related to your team — who they are, what they can access, when they work, and how they perform. Add staff members, assign granular role permissions, build weekly schedules, track real-time clock-ins, and measure individual performance KPIs that pull automatically from live POS data. Everything is connected: shifts feed into payroll, clock-ins link to POS orders, and performance data updates with every table served.

## Staff List & Profiles

Your full team lives in a single searchable dashboard. Each staff card shows the member's name, role badge, and a live online status dot so you can see at a glance who is currently active on the system.

- Add new staff members and assign their role immediately
- Search by name or filter by role and status
- Live online status indicator per staff member
- Switch between card view and compact list view
- Deactivate a staff member instantly to remove their access

<Note>
  **Deactivating a staff member does not delete their data.** All historical records — orders, pay slips, attendance logs, and performance data — are preserved and remain accessible. Reactivate them at any time to restore full access.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Only restaurant owners can elevate a staff member to the manager or owner role. Managers cannot promote other staff members beyond their own role level. This prevents privilege escalation and keeps your system secure.
</Warning>

## Role Templates & Permissions

DishInk uses a granular permission system with individual toggles across every module. Rather than creating permissions from scratch per staff member, you build reusable **Role Templates** — a named permission set that you can assign to many staff members at once. Update the template once and every staff member using it inherits the change immediately.

**Permission categories covered:**

| Category | What you control |
|---|---|
| **Module access** | POS, KDS, Waiter App, Tables, Inventory, Analytics, Payroll |
| **Admin pages** | Dashboard, Menu, Gallery, Messages, QR, Orders, Content — view or edit per section |
| **Management controls** | Staff management, shift editing, payroll approval, tip distribution |
| **Transaction overrides** | Process payments, apply discounts, void orders |
| **Analytics sub-access** | Kitchen, Revenue, Menu, Staff analytics — each toggled independently |
| **Settings & Billing** | Contact details, opening hours, social links, terminals, subscription — explicit grant only |
| **LAN access** | LAN Monitor page visibility |

<Tip>
  Give your analytics-only managers access to specific analytics tabs without granting them POS or payroll access. Analytics permissions are fully independent — see the [Analytics](/operations/analytics) page for the four permission flags.
</Tip>

## PIN-Based Clock In/Out

Staff clock in and out from any device — tablet, phone, or desktop — using a secure 4–6 digit PIN. No app download required. The live shift timer starts the moment they clock in and is linked directly to their POS orders and assigned tables, giving managers a real-time view of who is on the floor and what they are handling.

**PIN management:**

- PINs are sent automatically to the staff member's email when their account is created
- Managers can reset a PIN by sending a temporary PIN to the staff email
- Managers can also set the PIN manually so the staff member can log in immediately
- Staff can change their PIN after first login

**Live shift feed** — the Shift Feed shows every active and completed session in real time. For each active staff member you see their role, time on duty, orders taken so far, and revenue served. Completed sessions are logged below with their total hours and output.

## Shift Scheduler

Schedule your team on a weekly grid with full visibility of each staff member's week. Assign a shift by selecting the staff member, date, start time, end time, and optional notes. Shifts are colour-coded by status so the whole week is readable at a glance.

| Status | Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **Active** | Green | Staff member is currently clocked in on this shift |
| **Scheduled** | Blue | Shift is assigned and upcoming |
| **Done** | Grey | Shift is complete and hours are logged |
| **Cancelled** | Red | Shift was cancelled (hours not counted) |

Navigate forward and backward through weeks, and add shift notes for context (e.g. "Covering James" or "Early close"). Schedules update in real time — staff see the latest version the moment you save a change.

## Staff Performance Analytics

Every staff member's performance data is tracked automatically from POS activity. No manual entry, no separate reports to run — the numbers update with every order taken and every table served.

**KPIs tracked per staff member:**

- **Revenue served** — total value of orders the staff member took
- **Orders taken** — total number of orders processed
- **Hours worked** — pulled from clock-in/clock-out records
- **Top-selling item** — the most frequently ordered item on their shifts
- **Average order value** — revenue served divided by orders taken

Filter performance data by **this week**, **this month**, or **all time**. Each staff member's card includes a progress bar toward a configurable revenue target, and top performers automatically receive a **high performer badge** to recognise and motivate your best team members.

<Info>
  Performance data is visible to managers by default. Staff members can view their own performance from their personal dashboard, but cannot view other team members' data unless explicitly granted the `can_view_analytics_staff` permission.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I set different permissions per staff member?">
    Yes. Every permission in DishInk is a granular toggle — you can turn individual access rights on or off per staff member. Use Role Templates to apply a standard permission set across multiple staff members, then adjust individual toggles on any member where you need a different configuration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is a role template?">
    A Role Template is a saved, reusable permission set with a name (e.g. "Senior Waiter", "Kitchen Lead", "Bar Staff"). Configure the permissions once, save the template, and apply it to any number of staff members. When you update the template — for example, adding KDS access to all Kitchen Lead roles — every staff member assigned that template inherits the change immediately. No need to update each staff member individually.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do staff clock in?">
    Staff clock in from any device using their secure 4–6 digit PIN. They open DishInk, enter their PIN on the clock-in screen, and their shift starts immediately. The system records the exact timestamp and links the session to their POS activity and assigned tables. No dedicated hardware or app download is required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I track who's currently working?">
    Yes. The live Shift Feed on the Staff Management dashboard shows every staff member currently clocked in, their role, how long they have been on duty, orders taken, and revenue served in the current session. Completed sessions from earlier in the day are listed below. Managers see this view in real time from any device.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is staff performance measured?">
    Performance KPIs are pulled automatically from POS data — no manual entry. The system tracks revenue served, total orders taken, hours worked (from clock-in records), top-selling item, and average order value per staff member. You can filter by week, month, or all time, and set individual revenue targets with a progress bar tracker.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
