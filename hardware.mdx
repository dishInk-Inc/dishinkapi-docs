## Device Requirements

If you are using your own devices rather than DishInk hardware, make sure they meet these minimums:

| Requirement | Minimum | Recommended |
|---|---|---|
| Operating system | Android 8.0+ | Android 10+ |
| RAM | 2 GB | 3 GB or more |
| Primary connectivity | WiFi or mobile data | WiFi |
| LAN Mode connectivity | WiFi (required) | WiFi |
| iOS / iPad | Available on request | — |

<Note>
  WiFi is required for **LAN Mode** — the feature that lets your devices talk to each other locally when the internet drops. Cloud sync works on either WiFi or mobile data bundles.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Do I need to use DishInk hardware to run the software?">
  Not at all. DishInk OS runs on any modern Android, Windows, or iOS device. You can use your own phones, tablets, or terminals and get the full DishInk experience. The hardware range exists for businesses that want a fully pre-configured, tested, and branded setup out of the box — it is never a requirement.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do DishInk devices come pre-configured?">
  Yes. Every device sourced through DishInk is pre-installed and configured with DishInk OS during your onboarding process. Your team can start using it on day one with no technical setup required.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is iOS or iPad supported?">
  DishInk's primary hardware range is Android and Windows-based. If you prefer iOS devices such as iPads, the team can source them for you. Mention it in your quote request and they will provide options, pricing, and availability for an iPad-based setup.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I mix my own devices with DishInk hardware?">
  Yes. You can run DishInk OS across a mix of DishInk-supplied hardware and your own compatible devices on the same network. All devices communicate seamlessly as long as they are connected to the same WiFi network. There is no lock-in and no compatibility issues.
</Accordion>
