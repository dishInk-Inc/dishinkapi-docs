## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is DishInk?">
    DishInk is an **all-in-one restaurant operating system** built for African and Zambian hospitality businesses. It brings 12 modules together into one connected platform: POS, Kitchen Display (KDS), Waiter App, Table Management, Inventory & Food Costing, HRM & Payroll, Staff Attendance, Analytics, Online Ordering, QR Digital Menu, Website, and LAN & Offline Mode. Whether you run a neighbourhood café or a multi-location chain, every part of your operation — from the moment a customer orders to the moment payroll runs — is handled in one place.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
    Setup time depends on your plan. On the **Starter** plan, you can self-setup and go live in under 5 minutes. The **Growth** plan with done-for-you onboarding is typically completed within 1–2 business days. The **Operations Suite** requires a full deployment — including POS configuration, KDS routing, inventory and recipe setup, staff accounts, and on-site training — which takes **3–5 business days** depending on the size of your venue. **Enterprise** setup timelines are agreed during your discovery call.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need technical knowledge to use DishInk?">
    No. DishInk is built for restaurant owners and operators, not developers. The dashboard is fully no-code and designed to be intuitive from day one. For the Operations Suite and Enterprise plans, the DishInk team handles all configuration during onboarding and provides hands-on training for your managers, cashiers, waiters, and kitchen staff — so your whole team knows exactly how to use the system before you go live.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes. Every plan includes a **5-day free trial** with no credit card required. Sign up at [dishink.com/auth?mode=signup](https://dishink.com/auth?mode=signup) and your dashboard is ready instantly. You can explore all the features available on your chosen plan track before committing to a subscription.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after the trial ends?">
    After your 5-day trial, you choose a paid plan to continue. All your data — menu items, settings, staff accounts, and configurations — is preserved when you upgrade. If you do not select a plan, your account goes inactive but your data is retained for 90 days. You can reactivate at any time during that window without losing anything.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## POS & Payments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods does the POS support?">
    The DishInk POS supports **cash, card, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Zamtel Kwacha, and split payments**. You can split a single bill across multiple payment types — for example, part cash and part mobile money. Every payment method is recorded per transaction and flows directly into your sales reports.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the DishInk POS ZRA compliant?">
    Yes. The DishInk POS is fully **ZRA (Zambia Revenue Authority) compliant** and built to meet the requirements of the Smart Invoice system. Every sale is properly recorded and receipts are issued in the correct ZRA format. There are no manual tax calculations and no separate ZRA software required — compliance is built in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does DishInk process card payments directly?">
    No. DishInk records card transactions in your system but does not process card payments itself. Your existing card payment provider handles the actual card processing. DishInk logs the transaction, links it to the order, and includes it in your reports — your provider handles the money movement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I run multiple POS terminals at the same time?">
    Yes. The DishInk POS supports **unlimited simultaneous terminals** on the same network. You can run a main cashier POS, a bar terminal, and one or more server terminals at the same time — all synced in real time. Orders from any terminal fire instantly to the KDS and appear in your backend dashboard immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the POS work offline?">
    Yes. The POS continues operating even when your internet drops, using two layers of protection: **LAN mode** (orders sync across all devices on your local WiFi network) and **local storage** (data is saved on the device itself if even WiFi is unavailable). Everything syncs automatically the moment your connection is restored — no manual steps required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Offline & Connectivity

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is LAN mode and how does it work?">
    LAN mode is DishInk's second offline safety net. When your internet connection drops, your POS, KDS, and Waiter App continue communicating with each other over your **local WiFi network** — no internet required. Orders placed on the POS still fire to the kitchen display in real time, and all data stays in sync across every device on your network. As long as all your devices are on the same WiFi router, service continues uninterrupted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when the venue is completely offline — no internet and no WiFi?">
    If both internet and WiFi are unavailable, DishInk's third safety net activates: **local storage**. Every device saves all data locally as it is entered. When connectivity is restored — whether WiFi first or internet — the system automatically syncs all stored data to the cloud. You never lose an order, payment, or transaction, even during a total outage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I lose any data if my internet drops mid-service?">
    No. DishInk uses a three-layer offline architecture: cloud sync (normal operation), LAN sync (WiFi only), and local device storage (fully offline). Data is always captured at the device level first, then propagated upward as connectivity allows. Nothing is lost when the internet drops.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What hardware do I need to use offline mode?">
    No special hardware is required. Any standard WiFi router is sufficient for LAN mode. DishInk runs on any modern Android or iOS device, tablet, or touchscreen monitor. There is no requirement to purchase dedicated offline hardware from DishInk or any other supplier.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Hardware

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my own devices with DishInk?">
    Yes. DishInk is fully **hardware agnostic**. The software runs on any modern Android tablet, Android phone, iPad, iOS device, Windows touchscreen, or Mac. You can use your existing devices, purchase hardware independently, or source pre-configured hardware from DishInk. There is no requirement to buy from us — as long as your device meets the minimum specifications, DishInk will run perfectly on it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the minimum hardware requirements?">
    For the best experience, DishInk recommends devices running **Android 8.0 or later** (or equivalent iOS version) with **3 GB RAM or more**. Lower-spec devices may work for lighter use cases, but 3 GB+ RAM ensures smooth performance during peak service, especially when running the POS, KDS, or Waiter App simultaneously on the same device.

    Yes. All hardware purchased through DishInk — POS terminals, KDS screens, waiter tablets, waiter phones, and thermal printers — comes **pre-configured and ready to run** DishInk OS. You do not need to set anything up on the device. DishInk delivers hardware across Zambia and internationally.
