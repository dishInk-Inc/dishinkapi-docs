DishInk gives you a branded restaurant website at no extra cost — fully synced with your menu, updated instantly from the dashboard.

## Overview

Every DishInk account includes:

- Custom domain support (yourrestaurant.com)
- Auto-updating menu tied to Menu Items
- Gallery, contact info, opening hours, map
- SEO-friendly pages
- Mobile-optimised layout

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Set up your website even if you have another site. DishInk auto-syncs the menu so at least one always stays current.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not create parallel menus — always drive from Menu Items so POS, KDS, QR, and website stay perfectly in sync.
</Warning>
