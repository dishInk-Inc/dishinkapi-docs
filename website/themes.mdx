Themes control the visual identity of your website — colours, typography, layout, and imagery. Switch themes with a single tap.

## Overview

DishInk ships with themes matched to venue type:

- **Bistro** — warm, casual dining
- **Fine dining** — elegant, restrained
- **Café** — bright, welcoming
- **Bar** — dark, moody
- **Fast food** — bold, energetic

Custom brand colours can be applied on top of any theme.

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Match the theme to venue reality, not aspiration. A café in a fine-dining theme looks pretentious.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not use more than two brand colours. Extra colours dilute recognisability.
</Warning>
