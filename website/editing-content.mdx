The website content editor lets you update hero text, about copy, opening hours, and calls-to-action without a web developer.

## Overview

Editable sections:

- Hero (banner text and image)
- About (story, values, chef bio)
- Opening hours
- Location and map
- Contact form
- Call-to-action buttons (book a table, order on WhatsApp)

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Keep hero text under 12 words. Long headlines lose readers on mobile.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not paste text from Word. Hidden formatting breaks the layout — paste as plain text and re-format inside the editor.
</Warning>
