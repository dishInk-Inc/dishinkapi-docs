Contact information appears on every page and drives reservations, deliveries, and returning customers.

## Overview

Contact fields:

- Phone (with WhatsApp deep link)
- Email
- Physical address with map pin
- Opening hours
- Social profiles (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)

<Note>
  The WhatsApp deep link opens directly into your account. If you use WhatsApp ordering, the link auto-fills a menu greeting.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Update opening hours before public holidays. Nothing frustrates a customer more than closed doors at posted hours.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not list a personal WhatsApp number. Use a business account so shifts can share access.
</Warning>
