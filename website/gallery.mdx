The gallery showcases your restaurant — food photography, interior shots, event moments, chef stories.

## Overview

Gallery images appear:

- On the website home page
- On dedicated gallery pages
- In social preview cards when shared

## Manage the gallery

<Frame caption="Gallery manager">
  ![Gallery](/images/website/gallery.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Large images are auto-compressed for mobile. Upload the highest resolution you have.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Refresh the gallery quarterly. Stale photos signal a stale restaurant.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not upload blurred or under-lit photos. Poor gallery images hurt more than helping.
</Warning>
