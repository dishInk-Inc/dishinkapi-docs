QR Menus let guests view your menu on their own phones by scanning a table sticker. No app download, no login, no wait — just scan and browse.

## Overview

Each table has a unique QR code that opens a mobile-optimised digital menu. Optional add-ons:

- Order from the phone — waiter approves before firing to kitchen
- Call waiter button
- Bill request button
- Multi-language menu

</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Print QR stickers on tabletop-safe laminated cards. Cheap stickers peel off after weeks of cleaning.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not use one QR code for every table. Table-specific QRs enable one-tap bill requests and waiter calls.
</Warning>
