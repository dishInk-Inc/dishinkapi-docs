Clock in at the start of every shift. This records the opening float, links every subsequent action to the staff member, and starts payroll time tracking.

## Overview

Clock-in captures:

- Staff name and role
- Terminal and branch
- Opening cash float (for cashiers)
- Timestamp — the payroll start

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Count the drawer with a witness. Two-person counts eliminate almost all opening-float disputes.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not clock in on behalf of a co-worker. Every action after clock-in is tied to your PIN.
</Warning>
