Clock out at the end of every shift. This closes the session, reconciles cash, and generates the shift report.

## Overview

Clock-out captures:

- Closing cash count and variance
- Total time worked
- Any pending open orders (must be resolved before clocking out)

  ![Clock out](/images/staff/clock-out.png)

<Note>
  Managers can require a supervisor PIN for variances above a configurable threshold.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Cash out and clock out at the same station. Splitting the two flows across terminals causes drawer mismatches.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not clock out with open bills on your name. Reassign or close first — orphaned bills disrupt reports.
</Warning>
