Every user in DishInk has a staff account with an assigned role, branch access, and PIN. Owners and administrators create and manage accounts under **Staff Management**.

## Overview

A staff account controls:

- Login credentials (email \+ password, PIN, or both)
- Role and permissions
- Assigned branches
- Pay rate and payroll rules
- Personal details for shift reports

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Assign roles by job function, not by seniority. "Head chef" is a schedule label, but their DishInk role should be **Kitchen** or a custom role.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not share accounts. Every audit trail entry attaches to the account, so shared accounts destroy accountability.
</Warning>
