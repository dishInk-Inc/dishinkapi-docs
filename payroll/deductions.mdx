## Overview

**Deductions** subtract from gross pay before net pay is calculated. DishInk supports statutory Zambian deductions (PAYE, NAPSA) and custom deductions (uniform, meals, loans, etc.). Deductions can be applied via **preset packages** to your entire team or individually per staff member.

## Standard Zambian deductions

### PAYE (Pay-As-You-Earn)

- Applies to gross pay above the ZRA-published threshold.
- Rate varies by band; the **Zambia Standard** preset applies a **25%** rate on the taxable portion.
- Check current bands with ZRA before each financial year.

### NAPSA (National Pension Scheme Authority)

- **5%** of gross pay, capped at the current NAPSA ceiling.
- Employer contribution matched separately (not in this deduction — tracked in accounting).

<Note>
  DishInk uses the rates configured in your preset at the moment of payroll generation. **Update presets** when ZRA or NAPSA revise their rates.
</Note>

## Applying a preset

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Deductions tab">
    Go to **Payroll → Deductions**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a preset">
    Select **Zambia Standard**, **Basic**, **Full Package**, or **Hospitality**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview">
    See which staff members are affected and the estimated impact per pay slip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply to all or selected staff">
    Apply to the whole team, or check specific staff members to include only them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Custom deductions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Deduction">
    From the Deductions tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the deduction">
    - **Label** — e.g. "Uniform allowance", "Meal deduction"
    - **Type** — fixed amount or percentage of gross
    - **Amount / %**
    - **Recurrence** — every period, one-off, or until a target reached (for loans)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to staff">
    Pick one staff member, a group by role, or all staff.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Deduction applies to the next generated payroll record for those staff.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Per-staff overrides

From the **Staff Overview** row on the Deductions tab, you can:

- Add a deduction specific to that staff member
- Remove a preset deduction for that staff member (e.g. exempt a minor from PAYE)
- Set an end date for temporary deductions

## Order of application

Deductions are applied in this order on the pay slip:

1. **Statutory** — PAYE, NAPSA
2. **Salary advance repayments** — outstanding approved advances
3. **Custom fixed** — uniform, meals, one-off items
4. **Custom percentage** — percentage-based custom items

`Net pay = Gross − sum of all deductions`

<Note>
  If **allow negative net pay** is off ([Payroll Settings](/payroll/settings)), deductions stop at zero and the unpaid remainder rolls to the next period.
</Note>

## Tips

- Use **Zambia Standard** as your baseline — covers 90% of registered employers.
- Keep **custom labels short and clear** — "Uniform" is better than "UF-2024-Q1".
- Review deductions **once a year** with your accountant to catch rate changes.
- For **loan-style deductions**, use "until target reached" so the deduction stops automatically once the loan is repaid.

## Common mistakes

- **Applying PAYE to staff below the threshold.** Waste and inaccurate — use the Basic preset instead.
- **Duplicate deductions.** Applying a preset **and** adding NAPSA custom results in double NAPSA — always preview first.
- **Percentage on gross without cap.** A very high-earning staff member can have unrealistic NAPSA — set a cap.
- **Deleting a mid-loan deduction.** Won't reverse past pay slips. Use an end date instead.
