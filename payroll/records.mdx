## Overview

Every pay slip in DishInk is a **payroll record** with a lifecycle: **draft → approved → paid → disputed**. Records are stored per staff member per period. Managers can filter by status, period, or staff on the **Records** tab of the Admin Payroll page.

## Lifecycle states

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Draft" color="#6b7280" icon="pen">
    Just generated. Editable. Not visible to staff. Can be regenerated freely.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Approved" color="#2563eb" icon="circle-check">
    Amounts locked. Visible on the staff earnings page. Awaiting payment.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Paid" color="#16a34a" icon="money-bill-transfer">
    Marked as disbursed. Staff can download the pay slip PDF. Final state under normal flow.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Disputed" color="#dc2626" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Flagged by staff or admin. Under investigation. Amounts locked pending resolution.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Moving through the lifecycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Draft → Approved">
    On the record row, click **Approve**. Verify the totals, confirm. The record becomes visible to the staff member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approved → Paid">
    After disbursing funds (bank transfer, mobile money, cash), click **Mark Paid**. The record is now final.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approved / Paid → Disputed">
    Either the staff member (from their earnings page) or an admin can flag the record. Add a dispute reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disputed → Approved / Paid">
    Investigate, adjust if needed, and re-approve or mark paid. The dispute reason and resolution are stored on the record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reverting to Draft">
    An approved record can be reverted to draft (admin only) to fix an error. Paid records typically cannot be reverted — create an adjustment instead.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What a record contains

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Staff member** | Owner of the record |
| **Period start / end** | Date range covered |
| **Regular hours / OT hours** | For hourly wage types |
| **Base pay / OT pay / commission** | Gross earnings breakdown |
| **Tips** | Distributed pool share or direct-to-waiter total |
| **Deductions** | Line-by-line list with amounts |
| **Advances** | Outstanding advance balance applied |
| **Gross pay** | Base \+ OT \+ commission \+ tips |
| **Net pay** | Gross − deductions − advances |
| **Status** | draft / approved / paid / disputed |
| **Generated at / approved at / paid at** | Audit timestamps |

## Filters

The Records tab supports filtering by:

- **Period** — last 7 days, last 30 days, custom range
- **Status** — draft, approved, paid, disputed (or all)
- **Staff member** — single staff search
- **Net pay range** — top, middle, low, or negative

## Tips

- **Approve records in batches** — use a session to review all drafts, then approve as a block.
- **Mark Paid the same day** funds are sent so the audit trail matches reality.
- Keep **dispute reasons specific** — "Missing overtime for 5 April shift" is easier to resolve than "Wrong pay".
- Export **paid records as CSV** monthly for accounting and tax filing.

## Common mistakes

- **Leaving records in draft.** Staff can't see draft records — approve them so they appear on the earnings page.
- **Marking paid before actual disbursement.** Creates confusion if a bank transfer fails. Mark paid only after money moves.
- **Deleting a disputed record.** Never delete disputed records — resolve them for the audit trail.
- **Regenerating an approved record.** Blocked by design; revert to draft first.
