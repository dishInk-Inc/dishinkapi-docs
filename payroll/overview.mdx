## Overview

The DishInk Payroll module turns clocked hours, wage structures, tips, deductions, and salary advances into approved, paid pay slips — for a single staff member or your entire team in one click. Managers work from the **Admin Payroll** page (6 tabs); staff see their own earnings on the **Staff Earnings** page (6 tabs) inside the staff dashboard.

Payroll runs against real clock-in data, so every shift, every hour, and every overtime minute is accounted for. Tips collected during service are pooled and distributed by rule. Statutory deductions (Zambian PAYE, NAPSA) can be applied per staff member or applied to the whole team using a preset.

## Staff Earnings — 6 tabs (self-service)

Every staff member sees their own earnings on the **Staff Earnings** page. No admin access needed.

| Tab | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Earnings** | Current period net pay, breakdown card, download pay slip PDF |
| **Wage** | Active wage structure, base rate, overtime rate, effective from date |
| **History** | All past pay slips — approved, paid, or disputed |
| **Tips** | Unpaid vs. paid tips (mode-aware: distributed pool or direct-to-waiter) |
| **Deductions** | Active deductions applied to this staff member |
| **Advance** | Request an advance, view balance, see repayment schedule |

## How a payroll period works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Staff clock in and out">
    Every clock-in / clock-out is recorded with a timestamp. Overtime is calculated per shift against your OT threshold hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tips accumulate in the pool">
    Card tips and cash tips captured at close-out feed the pool. `equal_split` shares to eligible roles; `to_waiter` pays the waiter who owned the table.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate payroll">
    From **Payroll → Records**, click **Generate**. Choose bulk (all active staff) or single-staff. Pick the period. The `payroll-operations` edge function builds the records.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review draft records">
    Every generated record starts as **draft**. Review hours, wages, tips, deductions, and net pay. Adjust wage or deductions if needed and regenerate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve">
    Move records from **draft** to **approved**. This locks the amounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay out">
    Mark records **paid** when funds are disbursed. Staff can now download their pay slip PDF.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle disputes">
    If a staff member flags a pay slip, it moves to **disputed**. Investigate, adjust, and re-approve.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What feeds each pay slip

<Frame>
  ![Pay slip breakdown showing base pay, overtime, tips, deductions, and net pay](/images/payroll/pay-slip-breakdown.png)
</Frame>

| Line | Source |
| --- | --- |
| **Base pay** | Wage structure × hours worked (or fixed monthly / commission) |
| **Overtime pay** | Hours above OT threshold × OT multiplier × base rate |
| **Tips** | Distributed pool share or direct-to-waiter total for the period |
| **Commission** | Commission % × attributed sales (if wage type is `commission`) |
| **Salary advances** | Outstanding advance balance deducted from gross |
| **Statutory deductions** | PAYE, NAPSA, or other custom deductions |
| **Net pay** | Gross − advances − deductions |

<Note>
  If `allow_negative_net_pay` is disabled in **Payroll Settings**, deductions and advances can never push net pay below zero — remaining balances roll into the next period.
</Note>

## Roles

- **Admin / Owner** — full access to all 6 admin tabs; approve, pay, dispute; configure settings.
- **Manager** — generate and review records; approve advances (up to configured cap); cannot modify wage structures unless permitted.
- **Staff** — read-only access to their own **Staff Earnings** page.

See [Permissions](/staff/permissions) for the exact permission keys.

## Tips

- Run payroll on a **fixed schedule** (weekly or monthly) — staff plan around it.
- Approve records **in draft first** so mistakes are caught before pay day.
- Use the **Zambia Standard** deduction preset if you're a registered employer — it handles PAYE and NAPSA in one click.
- Set **per-staff advance limits** to avoid surprise deductions on pay day.
- Export the **Reports CSV** every period for your accountant.

## Common mistakes

- **Approving records before reviewing hours.** Always spot-check hours-by-staff on the Reports tab.
- **Forgetting to set a wage structure** before generating payroll. Staff with no active wage will show K0 base pay.
- **Distributing tips before all shifts are closed.** Close every open shift first — tips still pending distribution won't appear.
- **Leaving records in draft.** Draft records don't show on the staff earnings page. Approve them so staff can see their pay.
