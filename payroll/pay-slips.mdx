## Overview

A **pay slip** is the human-readable view of an approved or paid payroll record. Every staff member can download their own pay slip PDF from the **Staff Earnings** page. Admins can download any pay slip from the **Records** tab.

<Frame>
  ![Sample pay slip PDF with all line items](/images/payroll/pay-slip.png)
</Frame>

## Anatomy

### Header

- Business name, address, TPIN
- Staff name, role, staff ID
- Pay period (start → end)
- Pay slip number
- Generation date

### Earnings section

| Line | Formula |
| --- | --- |
| **Regular hours** | Sum of shift hours up to OT threshold |
| **Overtime hours** | Sum of shift hours above OT threshold |
| **Base pay** | Regular hours × base rate (or daily/monthly equivalent) |
| **Overtime pay** | OT hours × OT rate (multiplier of base or absolute) |
| **Commission** | Commission % × attributed sales (if applicable) |
| **Tips** | Pool share or direct-to-waiter total for the period |
| **Gross pay** | Sum of the above |

### Deductions section

Each active deduction shown with its label and amount:

- **PAYE** — Zambian Pay-As-You-Earn tax
- **NAPSA** — National Pension Scheme contribution
- **Salary advance repayment** — outstanding advance balance
- **Custom deductions** — uniform, meals, other agreed items

### Net pay

```text
Net pay = Gross pay − Total deductions − Advance repayment
```

<Note>
  If **allow negative net pay** is disabled ([Payroll Settings](/payroll/settings)), net pay is capped at zero and any remaining balance rolls into the next period.
</Note>

### Footer

- Approval signature block (if configured)
- Business contact for pay slip queries
- Generation timestamp

## Downloading a pay slip

### As a staff member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Staff Earnings">
    From the staff dashboard, tap **Earnings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the period">
    Choose the current period or a past one from **History**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download PDF">
    Tap the download icon on the record. The pay slip opens as a PDF.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### As an admin

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payroll Records">
    Go to **Payroll → Records**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the record">
    Filter by staff or period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the download icon">
    Generates the pay slip PDF for the selected record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sending pay slips

Approved and paid pay slips are automatically visible on the staff earnings page. For staff who need email or WhatsApp copies:

1. Download the PDF.
2. Send via your normal channel.

Automated email/WhatsApp pay slip delivery can be enabled per venue — contact support.

## Tips

- Encourage staff to **check their pay slip on approval day** so disputes surface early.
- Store paid pay slips as **CSV export** at month-end for your accountant.
- If your logo isn't on the pay slip, upload it in **Business Settings**.
- Include **TPIN** on every pay slip if you're VAT-registered — add it in business settings.

## Common mistakes

- **Editing a pay slip PDF externally.** Never edit the PDF — always adjust the source record and regenerate.
- **Different totals on PDF vs. earnings page.** Usually means the record was regenerated after PDF download. Download the fresh PDF.
- **Missing tips on the pay slip.** Tips only appear after distribution is run. See [Tip Distribution](/payroll/tip-distribution).
