Every sale generates a receipt. DishInk prints ZRA-compliant receipts with QR codes, and supports paper, email, WhatsApp, and SMS delivery.

## Overview

Receipt content:

- Business name, address, ZRA taxpayer identification
- Order number and timestamp
- Line items with modifiers
- Subtotal, discounts, tax, service charge, total
- Payment breakdown
- ZRA Smart Invoice QR code (where applicable)

</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Offer digital by default, paper on request. Saves paper cost and keeps the environment happy.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not omit the ZRA QR code from receipts in VAT-registered mode. This is a Smart Invoice compliance requirement.
</Warning>
