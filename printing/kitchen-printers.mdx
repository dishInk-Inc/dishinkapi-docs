Kitchen printers deliver paper tickets in kitchens where a KDS is not used, or as a fallback to KDS screens. Different from receipt printers — they print orders, not bills.

## Overview

Kitchen printer routing:

- Route by category (drinks → bar printer, food → kitchen printer)
- Route by station (grill, fry, prep) — same logic as KDS station view
- Automatic reprint if not acknowledged within N minutes

<Note>
  Kitchen printers complement, not replace, the KDS. Many venues use both — printer as a paper backup.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Set the auto-reprint interval to 3 minutes. Rare but critical for busy kitchens where a paper ticket falls off the pass.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not run kitchen printer only — use it alongside a KDS. Paper is lossy; screens don't lose tickets.
</Warning>
