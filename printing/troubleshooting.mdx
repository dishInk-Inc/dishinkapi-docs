Printer issues cause the most day-to-day support tickets. This page walks through the top failures and their fixes.

## Overview

Common symptoms:

- Printer prints nothing
- Printer prints garbled characters
- Only some terminals can print
- Paper feeds but no ink appears
- Duplicate prints

## Diagnostic steps

<Frame caption="Printer diagnostics">
  ![Diagnostics](/images/printing/troubleshooting.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Thermal printers use heat, not ink. "Paper feeds but no printing" usually means the paper is loaded upside down.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Keep two rolls of paper at every printer and a spare printer for busy venues. Downtime is your enemy.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not clean thermal print heads with alcohol wipes carelessly — damaged heads print with faint lines forever.
</Warning>
