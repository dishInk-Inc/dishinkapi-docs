Categories organise your menu into logical groups — starters, mains, desserts, drinks, cocktails. Categories drive POS layout, category reports, and KDS routing.

## Overview

- Categories appear on the POS as tab buttons.
- KDS routing can be configured per category (e.g. cocktails go to the bar KDS).
- [Category Reports](/reports/category-reports) roll up sales by category.

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Order categories by service sequence — starters, mains, sides, desserts, drinks. Waiters build orders faster with a familiar layout.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not put products in multiple categories to boost visibility. Duplication skews category reports and confuses reporting.
</Warning>
