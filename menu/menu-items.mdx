Menu Items are the customer-facing products. Each has a name, description, image, price, modifiers, category, and (optionally) a recipe linking it to inventory.

## Overview

Every menu item can carry:

- Name and description
- Photo
- Base price and variant prices
- Modifier groups (extras, cooking preference)
- Category and KDS station
- Prep-time target
- Availability schedule

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Use consistent photo dimensions (square, 1:1). QR menus and websites render cleaner with uniform photos.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not publish menu items without descriptions on your QR menu — guests skip anything they don't recognise.
</Warning>
