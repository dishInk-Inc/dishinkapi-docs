Availability controls when a menu item can be sold — by day, hour, branch, or stock condition.

## Overview

Common availability patterns:

- Breakfast items — 6:00–11:00 only
- Sunday roast — Sundays only
- Cocktails — bar branches only
- Fresh fish — available while ingredient stock ≥ 5

<Note>

</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Use time-based availability instead of manually toggling items. Staff forget; schedules don't.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not disable availability by deleting a menu item. Deletion removes it from historical reports too. Use availability instead.
</Warning>
