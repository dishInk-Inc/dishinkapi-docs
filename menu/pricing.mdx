Pricing controls what customers pay. DishInk supports base prices, variant prices, modifier surcharges, time-based prices (happy hour), and location-based prices (per-branch overrides).

## Overview

| Price type | Use case |
| --- | --- |
| **Base price** | Standard menu price |
| **Variant price** | Small/large, glass/bottle |
| **Modifier surcharge** | Extra cheese \+ZMW 10 |
| **Time-based (happy hour)** | Discount from 5–7 pm |
| **Location override** | Different price in a premium branch |
| **Delivery price** | Higher for delivery orders |

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Review pricing quarterly against your food cost. A 30% ingredient cost jump erodes margins fast — catch it before it spreads.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not increase prices without informing the front line. Cashiers get caught explaining price jumps to regulars.
</Warning>
