Kitchen order issues typically involve missing tickets, wrong-station routing, or stale prep timers.

## Overview

Symptoms:

- Ticket sent from Waiter App but not visible on KDS
- Ticket appears at the wrong station
- Prep timer stuck at 00:00 or unrealistic times
- Ticket bumped twice

</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Confirm station routing after any menu update. New items sometimes land in the wrong category and route poorly.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not bump tickets from the KDS to "catch up" if the kitchen fell behind. Timing metrics become meaningless.
</Warning>
