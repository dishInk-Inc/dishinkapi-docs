Printer problems are the number-one operational disruption. Follow this diagnostic flow before calling support.

## Overview

Symptoms:

- Printer prints nothing
- Printer prints garbled
- Only some terminals can print
- Duplicate receipts
- KDS printer misses tickets

<Note>
  Thermal receipts fade with age. If historical receipts look faint, that is normal — not a printer fault.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Keep a spare paper roll at every printer and a spare printer for busy venues. Downtime kills service.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not use paper that isn't thermal. Regular paper feeds fine but produces no ink.
</Warning>
