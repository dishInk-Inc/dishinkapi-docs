Payment issues cover failed card transactions, mobile-money mismatches, cash variances, and unresolved partial payments.

## Overview

Symptoms:

- Card terminal declined but customer says it went through
- Mobile money not confirmed on POS
- Cash drawer variance at cash-out
- Split bill balance doesn't reach zero

<Note>
  DishInk records payments alongside your provider — it does not process money. Any discrepancy must be traced back to your provider's ledger, not DishInk.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Reconcile mobile-money receipts hourly during peak service. Waiting until end-of-day makes tracing individual transactions painful.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not refund a card payment as cash. Card payments must be reversed through the card terminal.
</Warning>
