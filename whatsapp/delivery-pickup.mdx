Configure delivery zones, pickup windows, minimum orders, and rider assignment for every online order source.

## Overview

Delivery config:

- Delivery zones (radius or drawn on a map)
- Delivery fees per zone
- Minimum order per zone
- Rider assignment (in-house or third-party)
- ETA calculation (kitchen load \+ travel time)

Pickup config:

- Pickup windows (e.g. every 15 min)
- Curb-side pickup instructions
- Advance-order support

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Start with two zones (near/far). Fine-grained zones create edge cases; two are usually enough.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not set minimum order too low. Delivery breaks even only above a certain basket size — calculate before publishing.
</Warning>
