Accept online payments for WhatsApp, QR, and website orders — mobile money, card, and pay-on-delivery.

## Overview

Supported methods:

- **Mobile money** — MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Zamtel
- **Card link** — payment page opens from WhatsApp
- **Bank transfer** — with reference code
- **Pay on delivery / pickup** — cash or card at handover

<Note>
  DishInk does not process payments directly — it records the transaction and links to your provider. See the note in the POS overview about payment providers.
</Note>

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Require prepayment for delivery, allow pay-on-collection for pickup. This balances risk and convenience.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not fulfil unpaid orders without a verified customer. Prepayment fraud is common in delivery — verify first-time customers.
</Warning>
