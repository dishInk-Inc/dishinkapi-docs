Connect a WhatsApp number, configure auto-replies, and publish your ordering menu.

## Overview

Two tiers of integration:

- **WhatsApp Business App** — free, suitable for smaller venues, one device at a time.
- **WhatsApp Business API** — required for high volume, multi-agent, and multi-branch. Verified sender.

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Keep the greeting under 3 lines. Long auto-replies bury the menu link.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not skip business-hours auto-reply. Orders after hours confuse customers and cause negative reviews.
</Warning>
