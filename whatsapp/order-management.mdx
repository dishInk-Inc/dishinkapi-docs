The Order Management queue is the unified view of every online order — WhatsApp, QR menu, and website — in one screen.

## Overview

Each order card shows:

- Source (WhatsApp / QR / Website)
- Order number and customer name
- Items, modifiers, notes
- Delivery, pickup, or dine-in
- Payment status
- Time since submitted

Orders are accepted, sent to the KDS, and tracked through to completion in the same flow as POS orders.

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Assign a dedicated staff member to the queue during peak hours. Split attention between counter and queue causes missed WhatsApp orders.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not accept orders without confirming stock. If you cannot fulfil, reject immediately — delayed rejections erode trust.
</Warning>
