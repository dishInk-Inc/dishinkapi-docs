Auto-replies handle greetings, menus, order confirmations, and out-of-hours messages so no customer waits for a human response.

## Overview

Auto-reply types:

- **Welcome greeting** — first message with the menu link
- **Business hours** — friendly note when closed
- **Order acknowledgement** — immediate confirmation when a cart is submitted
- **Status updates** — preparing, ready, out for delivery
- **Post-delivery** — thank you \+ review prompt

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Write auto-replies in the language your customers use. Bilingual variants work — don't guess.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not stuff auto-replies with marketing. Customers unsubscribe from over-messaging.
</Warning>
