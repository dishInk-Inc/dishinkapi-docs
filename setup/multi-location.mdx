DishInk's multi-location management lets you run every branch of your restaurant group from a single login — with live data sync across all locations, centralised reporting, and the same consistent workflows at every site. Whether you operate two branches or plan to scale to twenty, you get full visibility of your entire portfolio without switching between systems or consolidating data manually. Multi-location management is available on the **Operations Suite** and **Enterprise** plans.

## Plan Requirements

Your plan determines how many locations are included and what it costs to add more.

| Plan | Locations included | Extra locations | Maximum |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Operations Suite** | 2 | K750/mo per extra location | Up to 5 total (then upgrade to Enterprise) |
| **Enterprise** | 5 | K1,500/mo per extra location | Unlimited |

A "location" is any different physical address, concept, or brand operating under your group. Contact the DishInk team for a custom quote if you are planning a large-scale rollout.

## Features

### Centralised Dashboard

All your branches are visible from one login and one screen. Every transaction, stock movement, and staff clock-in from every location syncs to your central dashboard in real time — no batch imports, no waiting for end-of-day reports, no manual consolidation. You see what is happening across your whole business as it happens.

### Branch-Level Reporting

Every branch generates its own reporting stream — revenue, order volume, food cost, and average spend per cover — so you always know exactly how each location is performing on its own. Side-by-side performance comparison reports let you identify which branches are thriving, which need attention, and where your best growth opportunities are. All reports are accessible remotely from any device.

### Unified Staff Management

Staff accounts, roles, and permissions are managed from one place across all branches. Add a new team member to any location, update their role, or adjust their access level without logging into a separate system for each site. Payroll for all locations runs from the same single view — shifts, attendance, tips, advances, and deductions across every branch calculated and ready to approve in one action.

### Inventory Per Branch

Each branch tracks its own stock independently. When an item sells at any terminal in any location, the inventory for that branch deducts automatically based on the recipe linked to that item. Low-stock alerts are configured per branch so your managers at each site are notified when key ingredients fall below threshold — without that noise reaching every other location.

### Consistent Operations

Every branch runs the same DishInk software, the same menu configuration, and the same operational workflows. Customers get an identical experience at every location. New staff are trained once on a system they already know from any other branch. And any update you make to shared menus or settings rolls out consistently across every site.

## Adding a New Location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Branches">
    In your DishInk dashboard, navigate to **Settings → Branches** and select **Add New Branch**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the branch details">
    Fill in the new branch's name, physical address, and contact information. These details appear on the branch's public-facing website and are used in branch-level reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure modules for the branch">
    Enable the operational modules the new branch will use — POS, Kitchen Display (KDS), Waiter App, Inventory, and so on. You can configure the branch to match your existing locations or set it up differently based on how that site operates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add staff accounts for the branch">
    Go to **Staff Management** and create accounts for the team at the new branch. Assign roles and set PINs. Staff at the new branch are immediately visible in your unified staff management view alongside everyone else in the group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact DishInk for hardware setup and training">
    Reach out to the DishInk team at dishink26@gmail.com or +260 973 171 744 to coordinate on-site hardware setup and staff training for the new branch. Enterprise customers receive dedicated onboarding per branch as part of their plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can branches operate independently if the internet drops?">
    Yes. Each branch runs DishInk's LAN and Offline Mode, which keeps the POS and Kitchen Display operational over the local network even when the internet goes down. Orders keep firing to the kitchen, payments keep processing, and all data syncs centrally the moment the connection is restored. Your service at any branch is never interrupted by connectivity issues.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I compare performance across branches?">
    Yes. DishInk's branch comparison reports give you side-by-side analytics for every location — revenue, order volume, food cost, average spend per cover, and staff performance metrics. These reports are accessible from your central dashboard and update in real time as sales come in across all your sites.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What plan do I need to manage multiple locations?">
    You need the **Operations Suite** plan (K4,500/mo) to manage 2 locations. You can add up to 3 extra locations at K750/mo each, giving you a maximum of 5 locations on Operations Suite. If you need 5 or more locations, or want dedicated account management and enterprise-level support, upgrade to **Enterprise** (K9,500/mo), which includes 5 locations and allows unlimited expansion at K1,500/mo per extra location.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can different branches have different menus?">
    Yes. While DishInk gives you the ability to maintain consistent menus across all branches, each branch can also be configured with its own menu items, categories, pricing, and availability. This is useful for chains where locations offer regionally specific items or different menu sizes. All branch menus are still managed centrally from your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
