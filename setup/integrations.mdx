DishInk is designed to work alongside the tools your hospitality business already relies on. For booking platforms, the integration is entirely zero-code — you paste a URL and a "Book Now" button appears on your website and QR menu automatically. DishInk also supports accounting integrations for financial sync and a built-in delivery configuration for WhatsApp ordering. This page covers every integration available and how to set each one up.

## Booking Platform Integration

Connecting your preferred reservation platform takes three steps and requires no developers, no API keys, and no code of any kind.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your DishInk Dashboard">
    Log in at [https://dishink.com/auth](https://dishink.com/auth) and go to your management dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your booking link">
    Navigate to **Settings → Bookings**. Copy the direct booking URL from your reservation platform — this is the link your guests use to make a reservation — and paste it into the booking link field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and go live">
    Hit **Save**. A **"Book Now"** button appears automatically on your website and QR menu immediately. No further configuration is needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Linking vs Embedding

<Tip>
  DishInk recommends **linking** to your booking platform rather than embedding it in an iframe. Linking consistently delivers a better experience across every device and context:

  - **Best mobile responsiveness** — the booking platform loads in its own full-screen view on phones
  - **Faster loading** — no iframe overhead or cross-origin delay
  - **Proper QR scanning behaviour** — the booking link opens cleanly when a customer scans your QR code
  - **Better SEO** — linked pages are indexed and discoverable; iframed content is not

  The recommended approach is to let DishInk add the "Book Now" button automatically via Settings, and leave the link to open the booking platform directly.
</Tip>

## Yango Delivery Integration

If you use **WhatsApp Ordering**, you can configure how delivery is handled directly from your DishInk settings. DishInk supports two delivery modes:

| Mode | How it works |
|---|---|
| **We Handle** | Your restaurant creates the Yango delivery order on the customer's behalf after receiving their WhatsApp order |
| **Customer Handles** | The customer arranges their own Yango delivery or collection after placing their WhatsApp order |

To configure your delivery preference, go to **Settings → WhatsApp Ordering** and select the mode that matches how your venue operates. Your choice is displayed clearly to customers before they place an order so they know what to expect.

WhatsApp Ordering is available on **Growth, Operations Suite, and Enterprise** plans.
