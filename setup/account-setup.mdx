After you create your DishInk account, the next step is configuring your restaurant profile so that your website, QR menu, POS, and all connected modules reflect your brand and operate correctly from day one. This guide walks you through every setup step — from adding your restaurant details and uploading your logo to activating your subscription and getting your team ready to log in.

## Complete Your Restaurant Profile

Work through these steps in order after your first login. You can return to any section from your dashboard at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to your dashboard">
    Go to [https://dishink.com/auth](https://dishink.com/auth) and log in with the email address and password you used during signup. You land directly in the DishInk Management Portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your restaurant details">
    Navigate to **Settings → Restaurant Profile** and fill in your:
    - Restaurant name
    - Venue type (Restaurant, Café, Bar, or Fast Food)
    - Physical address
    - Phone number
    - Opening hours for each day of the week
    - Social media links (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, WhatsApp)

    These details appear on your website, About page, and map listing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo and set brand colours">
    Go to **Settings → Branding**. Upload your restaurant logo (PNG or JPG recommended) and pick your primary and accent brand colours. Your logo and colours apply instantly across your website, QR menu, and all customer-facing pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your website template">
    Under **Settings → Website → Template**, select the design that best fits your venue:

    | Template | Best for |
    |---|---|
    | **Modern** | Any venue — clean, minimal, works everywhere |
    | **Cafe Brew** | Coffee shops, brunch spots, and cosy cafés |
    | **Bold** | Upscale restaurants and premium dining |
    | **Nightclub** | Bars, lounges, and nightlife venues |

    You can switch templates at any time without losing any content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure payment methods">
    Go to **Settings → Payments** and enable the methods your venue accepts. DishInk supports:
    - Cash
    - Card
    - MTN MoMo
    - Airtel Money
    - Zamtel

    Enabled payment methods appear as options on your POS and are recorded against every transaction in your sales reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add staff accounts and set roles">
    Navigate to **Staff Management → Add Staff Member**. Create a profile for each team member and assign a role. Available roles include Cashier, Waiter, Kitchen Staff, Supervisor, and Manager. Each role has a defined permission set that controls what the staff member can see and do across the platform.

    See [Staff Login](#staff-login) below for how your team accesses the system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your subscription plan">
    Go to **Billing** to review and activate your plan. Your 5-day free trial begins automatically — no credit card is needed to start. When you are ready to go live permanently, choose the plan that matches your stage of growth.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Subscription Plans

<Info>

</Info>

| **Growth** | K599/mo | K850 (optional) | Established restaurants wanting a premium digital presence |

Setup fees for Starter and Growth are optional — you can self-setup for free. Setup fees for Operations Suite and Enterprise are required and cover full on-site configuration, hardware setup, and staff training.

## Staff Login

DishInk uses two separate login methods to keep manager access and staff access distinct and secure.

**Managers and owners** log in at [https://dishink.com/auth](https://dishink.com/auth) using their email address and password. This gives access to the full management dashboard, including settings, billing, analytics, payroll, and staff management.

**Staff members** use PIN-based login. On the login screen, switch to **"Staff PIN Login"** and enter the 4–6 digit PIN assigned to that staff member. Managers set and manage PINs from **Staff Management → [Staff Member Name] → Set PIN**.

PIN login is used for:
- Clocking in and out for shift attendance
- Logging into the POS terminal
- Accessing the Waiter App on shared devices

## Branding Customisation

Your branding settings control how your restaurant appears to customers across your website, QR menu, and all public-facing pages.

- **Logo** — upload from any device; displayed in your website header, QR code centre (Growth+), and social sharing previews
- **Brand colours** — set your primary and accent colours; applied across all templates automatically
- **Social links** — add Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and WhatsApp URLs; appear as icons in your website footer
- **DishInk badge** — shown in the footer on Starter and Growth plans; removed automatically on Operations Suite and Enterprise
- **Custom domain** — available as a one-time K2,000 add-on on Starter, Growth, and Operations Suite; included free on Enterprise
