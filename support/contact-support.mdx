Reach the DishInk support team by chat, WhatsApp, email, or phone. This page shows every channel, their hours, and the response times you should expect.

## Overview

| Channel | Response time | Hours |
| --- | --- | --- |
| WhatsApp | Under 90 minutes | 06:00–22:00 |
| Email | Under 24 hours | Business days |
| Phone (enterprise, Operations Suite) | Immediate | 24/7 |

## Contact support

  ![Support](/images/support/contact.png)

<Note>
  Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager — they respond to critical issues within 15 minutes, 24/7.
</Note>

## What to include

- Branch name and dashboard email
- Terminal type and OS
- Screenshot or video of the issue
- Time and rough transaction reference

## Tips & best practices

<Tip>
  Always include a screenshot. It cuts back-and-forth by half.
</Tip>

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
  Do not send sensitive information (PINs, passwords, card numbers) over WhatsApp or email. Use the secure in-app chat.
</Warning>
